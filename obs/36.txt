{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-01.jpg}}

Amelella, Yesu olliyowayekin satte, Petrona Yakonna Yohannena (Yohanneda Yesusi kudušemowanume) unutte kikano alya gerata dagadasi agunke. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-02.jpg}}

Yesu dagas kosimala, gadibiya uyende kibañake, sesiyana leledende leske, lagala kosima kwellokana woynawla lušos kolana kosimma leda leske. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-03.jpg}}

Woynalle Musette edessa Eliyatte mikkontike. Inna kwename galle muje ana lagala gon miname deya. Ime Yesutte Fanaka etetala Yerusalemala kišana baliyaññabbu koda kokoske. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-04.jpg}}
Musena Eliyana Yesutte koda kokosimala Petro Yesusi akeske, “alle godinasi kimesi maydandeya. Layte satte katana, ella Enasi, ella Musesi, okodoka ella Eliyasi.” Petro udamowa itakemme. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-05.jpg}}


Petro awroka kuda kosimala, dalama leledina yoki inarigikinolano dalamowa kasa kakin menella akeske, “ namema Akka Unubbu sus kasoma innama unusi fayyan keso.” olliye sattowaye anda kokkayloma sabbatasi lagala kokoyake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-06.jpg}}
Batti Yesu imesi tomiski, “mekkaylome. Femu”ske. Higidiyowalle limmala Yesu bilokanditta ellana kos kutamme. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-07.jpg}}


Yesuna olliye sattowayena alyowakin inke. Yesu fanakowalle olliyesi, “alle kominno mentimenaññabbu kwellokasina misasame. Aba eteta nabaleñña ide woynaña nakas fenanaña woyna kulabbu deya kwesi mesasana.”ske.


Matteo 17:1-9; Mark 9:2-8; Luka 9:28-36 kin kokama Anna awra fala.
