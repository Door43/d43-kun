

====== 50. Yesu Ide Koñña kišamowa. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-01.jpg?direct&}}

Galle angudabbu 2000 kišana kalla laga sasala kokosima kwa arte Yesu Messiyowa gogoda fala kotik gonke. Kristiane kotadana tawdoka kito goske. Yesu laga komaldala kide kona kišama amanidam kasoke. Yesu tamata  ide komma lusuma kišayyana, asoma amanidowa goññesuna. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-02.jpg?direct&}}
Yesu ide konowa kakoluda godimala, Anna keddusodabbu kakoludaki Unusi kigotama tabilabbu woyniyodinasi heske. Okodoka Mannodiyañña ellesi kasanasi heske. Yesu lagenalle woyniyos kinama fanakala, “ollañe, Anna mannoda  gogoda falena laga sasala  kokosima agara bubiyesi kisasa samon kišona, batti  komalda fanaka yona”ski udake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-03.jpg?direct&}}
Agara guja arte tamata Yesuñña kotikamme okoske. Yesu kulya baddata kogotamma anala Kristiyanesi gogoda falena šillay kotikamma kwesi fanen samon kišonasi kisasake. Unu, “biše ila fanaka iddi koke, woynabbu eyša gamu košere bubiyesi ollañe kenola!”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-04.jpg?direct&}}
Yesu inakedana kuda kisotake, “hara Andiyakin andanume. Lagena manne Abasi kakuma kokala, emesina sabbatañasi hayda kesoki eya famuna. Lagenalle  tagama anda miteyyana abanyomu, ayšisi Aba lagena manna Sadallasi nabat nafalkema. Eme Abasi amajonki ašik komaldata abanyomuyya batti Anna efuruna!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-05.jpg?direct&}}
Yesu olliyesi laga komalda kišana fanakowalle kwesi kišana damanañña keka kola kišonasi falena kisasake. Unu akeske,  “ kwella bišiyala elega mayda išiske. Niñisumala  badiyala abayiya iki elega maydowa baddasi tomma išis kiwi gaske. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-06.jpg?direct&}}

“Ibo kutama elega maydowa ilattema fanakala, kwowa hare, ‘Andaña, bišowalle ena nibomowa elega maydowa deya. No olle ayšisino tomma ilattemena?’nki okkelake. Andiya, ‘elega bayena abayam išiske’ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-07.jpg?direct&}}
“hariye andiyasi, ‘tommowa mama mawlanañambe?’nki okkelake. Andiya, ‘Ayya. Okon meminiya kina maydowana mewti famuna. Woynabbu ašik ila fanakata ewibbu itobbu batti tommowassi hummanki tomata fanketu, kinowassi šuwwañala weymu’”ski ifali kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-08.jpg?direct&}}

Olliye falowa abbara obinimme. Woynabbu keka kola kišonasi Yesusi okkelake. Yesu akeske, “elega mayda kibomowa Messiya deya, biša lagowa laga sasenassi kušurake, elega maydowa Anna mannodala kokosima kwe.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-09.jpg?direct&}}
“tommowa  abayowañña kosama kwesi, tomma išisumowa Sadallasi, ila fanakowa laga komaldasi, ila ilalowaye Anna maliyakesi  deya ušurama.” 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-10.jpg?direct&}}

“Laga komaldala maliyake Sadalla olliyesi ellala hummanki ucumma tomowatta fan kutuñña, olle gonki awnki hayda mema haymuñña. Woyna fanakowalle alale, Anna Iwa mannodala gonki uya leledende lemunaña.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-11.jpg?direct&}}
Okodoka Yesu laga komalda yomma ana Unu lagata kide kona kišama kudake. Gas kišimowa šadiya kokala kide koñña, yomala konta kontina  ulokowabbu, ide kuyala kokosima dalamebbu kide konaña. Yesu ide koma fanakala, obalema Kristiyane obalemekin okas fenki kulyala unutte kokoleñña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-12.jpg?direct&}}
Woyna fanakowalle woyniya kokosima Kristiane kulya baddata kokogotanaña, balakin femma akobinnette kokoleñña. Bubiye  Yesutte košañña. Woyna kulabbu Yesu agariyette sullumabbuna ellabbuna aritasi woyniyomunaña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu unusi kokobinime bubiyesi korona kišona amanida kišoke. Ime Annatte sulluma udumalala aritasi woyniyonki lugusomuñña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-14.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Anna Yesusi kokobinimma bubiyesi alalosunaña. Unu aritata  kucumma tomata fas kutubu aritasi imbabbna hayda memana kišana. Ay kišayyana ucunnima tomowa iñanaña, ide biyyena imesi kinynyi kiñanaña. 

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-15.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu ide kona fanakowa, Sadallasina mannodiyenna šillay kibal kinolanaña. Unu sadallasi aritata toma iñanasi Jehanna tomata fas kutuñña. Annasi fayyadakin Sadalla bada kosana kotawa košime bubiyesi unutte haymuñña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-16.jpg?direct&}}
Adum kime Hawatte Annasi fayyamummowabbu mangela lagata mikoke. Anna lagasi bada kosa kitareki lagena kibal kolanasi kimintike. Iša ditta Anna amelella dogolasuma kinammakulya tamana laga tama  kineneñña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-50-17.jpg?direct&}}

Adum kime Hawatte Annasi fayyamummowabbu mangela lagata mikoke. Anna lagasi bada kosa kitareki lagena kibal kolanasi kimintike. Iša ditta Anna amelella dogolasuma kinammakulya tamana laga tama  kineneñña.
Matteo 24:14;28:18; Yohanne 15:20, 16:33; Inta 2:10; Matteo13:24-30, 36-42; 1Tessalonika 4:13-5:11; Yakob 1:12; Matteo 22:13; Inta 20:10, 21:1-22:21 kin kokama Anna awra fala.
