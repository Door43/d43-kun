====== 37. Yesu Lazarosi Balakin Kiboma. ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-01.jpg}}


Amelella, Lazarosi sara anda kisarema edetta Yesuta kike. Lazarona osamiye bareda Mariyana Martana Yesu kodiya šadiye deya. Yesu inna kabarena kitikamala, “inna badena Anna ankatasi ditta balasimme”ske. Yesu kodiyenayesi anda  kimeyke, ditta kos kutama wagokowalle uye baresi kitaf goske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-02.jpg}}


Uye bare šasumala, Yesu olliyesi, “areb Yudeyata gadi,”ske. “Iša ditta Samina” olliye oksa koske, “uya angude anala sukowa agare eya famuna hen kolutake.” Yesu, “kodiñña lazaro atoda atoskeno atodakin nabondada”ske. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-03.jpg}}


Olliye Yesusi, “Lazaro atodale kišayya kale kišonamma iwi”nki okas kosoke. Ditta Yesu kekoka kisasaki, “Lazaro ibalke deya, eme mekobininasi aba olle nakos nawtammowabbu gogonake” ske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-04.jpg}}


Yesu woyna Lazaro gos kišima sukowatta kimala. Lazaro ibalemasi uye salle kiddike. Marta wakeke kiki Yesusi, “Andaña, ena alle nokos nutakessa masama Lazaro ibalenni diya, tamana Anna nokkelama data bubiya kesonowa nakobinke” ske




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-05.jpg}}

Yesu akeski ikas kišoke, “ Aba kasa fedana woyna goddana deya Ababbu kobinima kwa bubiya ibaleyana woyniya kišana. Abasi kobinima kwa ay kišayana ibalenni. Innasi nikobinibe?” Marta, “e, andaña! Ena Messiyya Anna kiša nišama nakobinke”ski ikas kišoke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-06.jpg}}

Badiyasi Marta yoki Yesu mindala koyaki, “O andaña, ena alle nokos nutakessa masama ibalenni diya”ski udake. Yesu, “Lazarosi inkalle mifalno” ski kokelake. Ime, “Nabulala deya, aw kinti” nki odake. Olle Yesu imbike!




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-07.jpg}}

Nabulowa ata nabulandeya, ide ugabbu kotoma kolutake. Yesu nabulowatta kimala, “aweki kamburda ugena ete” ske. Ditta marta, “Ibalemasi uye salle kiddike, tama šebra hallasuna”ske




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-08.jpg}}


Yesu, “abasi nokobiniya Anna ankata nintinanda nasasamme” ske. Batti  ugowa otutura kolake. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-09.jpg}}


Batti Yesu wiye kuya baddata galas liski, “O abba. Nitik kasomasi aftata nasoke. Ena dimoka nitik kasoma natakke. Iša ditta ena asamema inna alle lakan gomma kwenaye otakensi deya nawdama.”ske. batti mena andabbu, “Lazaro iša kawa.”ske




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-10.jpg}}

Woyna kulabbu Lazaro sesab kofirfitamiya kiša koke. Yesu, “kotada sesowa ebišiki sakan kewi”ski isasake. Inna faga minenabbu Yudekin arte Yesusi kokobinke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-37-11.jpg}}


Ditta Yude kobinna mare okkoluguke. Yesu kime Lazarotte ayken komin kiya famunissa matareba kolanasi kokobuceke.
