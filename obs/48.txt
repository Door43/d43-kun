====== 48. Yesu Yoññanda Kodama Messiya Deya ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-01.jpg?direct&}}

Anna laga kinnema fanakala data bubiya luša gos kinake Adumte Hawwatte ayniye bilela mikomey minake. Annasina mime minake badana balana kos kinamme. Anna laga akedoka kišanasi heske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-02.jpg?direct&}}

Sadalla Hawwa kutub Fogasunasi Eden Jarjinala darmabbu kudake. Woyna kulabbu Hawwatte Adumte Annala Mangela miminke. Imme mangela miminima sabababbu lagala kosima kwa bubiya ibadina balana kibalena kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-03.jpg?direct&}}

mišake. Inna fanakenakin kibinki Kwa bubiya mangela adaba kina košina kišake, okodoka Anna abaya kišake. Annatte kwatte agasala koskišima kotengenowa mangela sabababu komintike. Iša ditta Anna woyna kotengenowa kikas awsasunasi matareba kinake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-04.jpg?direct&}}

Hawwa košerekin ella sadalla ansanga sakosunasina, sadalla ocokoniyasi kinynyinowana amanita kišo kišike. Inna, Sadalla Messiyasi iya fasunowana Anna minda abarma kikas kiboññowana, Woynakin Messiya Sadallasi Aritata kiya sakkos kolana kišamowa kušuramandeya. Galla arte kulabbu Anna Yesu Messiya kišama keka kola kikoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-05.jpg?direct&}}

Anna lagasi biyabbu kiya kolama fanakowalle, Unusi kokobinime bubiye kufurunasi Arka sebbuske. Woyna tabilowa kokala, kwa iminima mangelowabbu Anna ibal kolandada, Iša ditta Anna Unusi kobinima bubiya kofurunasi Yesu sebbuske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-06.jpg?direct&}}

Galla muje artesi, kaše agare mangela sabababbu kokoyana akurowa kišob ontinasi mintitta Annasi fiteta koki koso koynakeke; innabbu .

Iša ditta fitetiya sanowabbu mangela imekin battan kolanasi šomumme. Yesu Kaše andiya andandeya.

Kaša aññadowayekin Aññadisuma damanowabbu lagala kosima kwa mangela bubiya kola fasunasi uliya tarbella fiteta kalla sugus kikike.

Yesu kwe mangele akura akura kikama sabbatasi kaše andiya tummandeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-07.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abrahamsi, “lagala kokosima košere enabbu šodina kosana”ski isasake. Yesu abrahama košera ndeya.

Yesubbu košere bubiye šodina kosake, ayšisi Yesusi kobinima kwa bubiya mangelakin kofuruma kišake, ide Aširmabbu Abrham košera kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-08.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abrahamsi ikka Isak kikoki Fiteta kimin kišonasi kudamam fanakowalle Anna Isak udabbu garma kiki kišoke.

Kime bubiññesi Mangeliññe sabbatasi bala kabalendada! Iša ditta Anna, kimesiddab fiteta kalla, Anna Garma, Yesu kasoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-09.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Eyitto dinesi komalda akura kiyaki Israel dinesi kinolanala garma kiša nace kiteyki itiya udidda garma kišowa kokobowa kofuluna kisasake.

Anna kokobowa kintimala uwa kiki ikka adigilesi iyammiya kiwi šaske. Innassi šada furdandada kokidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-10.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu šada furada Garmiñña ndeya. Unu lušana mangelittana ide šada furda fanakala koya fanke.

Kwella Yesubbu kokobinimala Yesu kokoba Kwowa mangela jiba kofalke deya. Anna akura oyna kowasi iwi šasuna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-11.jpg?direct&}}

Annam inatawa kikameda Israel agarette Anna ñelasa kuwake. Iša ditta tamma kwa bubiyatte ñelasa tama kuwake.

Woyna sabbatasi kosima agarakin kosima košerekin Yesusi kobinimabbu Anna agarette kokelanasi šosuna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-12.jpg?direct&}}

Muse Annawra fanesuma edessa anda gos kinake, ditta Yesu edesse bubiykin šasuma edessandeya.

Unu Annandeya, Woynabbu unu isanamana udama awra bubiyana Anna sanana Annawrana deya. Woynasi deya Yesu Anna Awranda kokidama.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-13.jpg?direct&}}

Anna lugusa Dawitsi košereyekin Ella lugusa nawlabbu Anna agarela Aritasi mannosuma kišananda ammanida kišoke.

Yesu Anna kišana Messiyana kišamowabbu aritasi mannosunasi šosuma Dawit košera katawamowa deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-14.jpg?direct&}}

Dawit Israel Lugusa gos kinake. Iša ditta Yesu laga bubiya Lugusandeya! Ide kide koki mannodiya alalabbuna sullumabbu aritasi mannosunaña.
