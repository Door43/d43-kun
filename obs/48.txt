====== Poulotte Silatte Filippi mimi samoma======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-01.jpg}}

Saulo Roma mannnoda waga bubiyata gaski solisuma fanakowalle, Roma dine kidanda “Paulo” dowa nafos kikke. Amelella Paulo kodiya Silatte Yesu gogoda fala salamunasi Filippi sukata mimike, sukowa kin adagala suba uda agare okobuca dagan gomma wagata mimike. Woyna fanakowalle darke  atarebella Lidiya da kokidama miteke. Unu Annasi kime gajosuma gos kinake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-02.jpg}}
Yesuñña kodama edettowa kibininasi Anna Lidiya urafa woykesso, ununa itiya dinena okobinke ide okodušeke. Paulotte Silattesi itiyala gomunasi kikidake. Immena Lidiyatte Itiya dinette ellala gonke




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-03.jpg}}

Paulo kimme silatte dagada wagowalle kwe artette kole koynake. Uyikelda olleb šammala aširma bayabbu kobinima kiša sabella imesi kotule kinake. Kiša sabena woyna unula kusukama aširmowabbu kwesi sula fas kišo kinake, ide andiye woynabbu mala koyte koynake. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-04.jpg}}

Saba kišowa badiya kisaki, “inna kenaye Anna andowa hare. Kofura tabila muda gnkeski” ski aws kinake. Ašik paulosi urfa kotokori kiyanata dayminda kide kudake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-05.jpg}}

komalda, kiša sabowa awdabbu kuda lemenasuma fanakala. Paulo Unuta magiski kišowalle kos kutam aširmowassi, “Yesu kidala unukin iša!” ske. Woynokala aširma bayowa kišowakin iwi kišake. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-06.jpg}}


 Kiša sabowa andiye anda kokošišinike! Ime  kišowa aširma bayowa kinattabu sula fas kišonnima kišamowano andiyowaye itida oyte kusummowa otakke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-07.jpg}}
Kišowa Andiye Paulotte Silattesi Roma mareta kikino imesi iyaki Mabusa itala kutuke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-08.jpg}}

Ime Paulotte Silattesi kasa kala kosima itowalle kikiki Midiyena elala kutu kililike. Iša ditta laga awda farka kiddimala Annasi Asara ananab  misaroke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-09.jpg}}

Kolalasi lagaišgida anda kišake! Mabuse ita dagge woyken kosake Mabuse bubiyekin lolena okotey kasake.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-10.jpg}}

Mabus ita alida atodakin targaske, mabusa ita dagge weyken kosamowa kintimala kayloke. Mabuse bubiye olajima Abbareski uliya koya fasunasi gegajiya akeda kiminina hesumena mabuse kiwib olajiya Roma antate oya famunowa kitakemasi deya. Paulo intiki, “Iwi! Uleyasi baya niminime. Bubañe Alle makoske”ski aws lemenas kisasake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-11.jpg}}

Mabusa ita alidowa Paulotte Silattete sugus kiki katodab, “nakofurunasi ay namini?”ski kokelake. Paulo, “Yesusi kobini Enana iteya Dinena mekofur mesanama” ske. Woynakin mabusa ita alidowa Paulo kimme Silattesi itiyata kikiki, šayšiye kinišeke. Paulo itowalle kolutame bubiyesi Yesu gogoda fala samos kišoke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-12.jpg}}
Mabusa ita alidowa itiya dinette Yesusi kokobinke ide kodušana kokke. Woynakin mabuse alidowa Paulo kime Silattesi ñada kola kišono miñake. išama damanowabbu bubiye samarab gogonke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-13.jpg}}

Aberkenattabu Filippila kokosima agarowaye mare Paulotte Silattesi mabusakin inolaki filippikin gamunasi kokelake. Paulotte silatte Lidiyana okosima kodiyellesina higin mintiki Sukowa miša ganke. Yesu gogoda fala saladoka salaske, ide košoda itana tawdoka kitafe kitoke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-48-14.jpg}}
Paulona Kristiana amara aññadena Yesu gogoda falowa suka arteta solinki samonki sasana kisasake. Okodoka košoda itela kokosima akobinnes  kititonasina isasanasina edetta arte kolata kišoke. Inna okolattama edettenaye Kitaba keddusa magade kosake. 


Apostle mina 16:11-40 kin kokama Annawra fala.
