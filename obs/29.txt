====== 29. Lugusa olliya Samanadittowa Fala. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella, Petro Yesusi, “Asasa, osamaña abasi fogas kaminiya minde aykalle samanan nawi?\\
Minde kontobarebe?”ski kokelake. Yesu, “Kontobarenume, kontobare minde šebkontobare\\
ditta.”ski ikas kišoke. Yesu inna kudamena kime bodesi dimala samanad kawindadowa deya.\\
Batti inna falena kifalike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu akeske, “Anna mannoda, lugusellam olliyette maliya kotada kiminina hesuma\\
kokalandeya. Olliyekin ella alufebbu kokelana šolda jiba anda kina kišike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-03.jpg?direct&}}

“Olliyowa jibiya jabisunasi šosummeno, lugusowa, ‘kwena jibiya jabis kolana šosummetti,\\
jibiye kifalenasi unusina itiya dinesina sabe kalla keta kenola’ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-04.jpg?direct&}}

“Olliyowa lugusa kekala tuski, ‘toro habanyoda kaso, naynama jibowa bubiya\\
jabinanañamma’ski ikas kišoke. Lugusowa olliyowassi nes kiyano inama jibiyana kiwi kišo\\
kisameno gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-05.jpg?direct&}}

“Ditta olliyowa Lugusowa kekakin battasuma kulabbu, kodiyowayekin ella šolda jiba\\
angudoka kinama harella kiteke. Samanas kowima kwowa, jiba kinama kodiyowassi ibinki,\\
“ninama jibañowa jabida kaso!” Ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-06.jpg?direct&}}

“Kodiya kišama harowa mindiya olkomala koyaki, “toro nakema hanyoda kaso, naynama jiba\\
bubiya jabin nasonañamma.”ske. ditta samanas kowima lugusa olliyowa hara kodiyowassi\\
jibiyowa jabis kišonata mabusa kutuke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-07.jpg?direct&}}

“lugusa olliya aññade išama damanowa kotikamala anda kokošišiniki lugusata koliki išama\\
damanowa kosasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-08.jpg?direct&}}

“Lugusowana harowassi ikida kikoki, “ena hara mongora! Jibeya nawi nasomowa nalon\\
daganumasi nawi nasoke. Enana okodoka niminina dadandeya.”ski lugusowa anda košišiniki\\
jibiya bubiya jabis kolanata mabusa kutuke.”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-09.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu woyna kulabbu, “emena uleyasi osameyesi samanan mewimma kišayya kulya baddala\\
kosima Awana samanas kewinni.”ski isasake.

Matteo 18:21-35 kin kokama Anna awra fala
