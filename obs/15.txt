====== 15. Ñelasa Lagowatta Koluma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-01.jpg?direct&}}

komaldala Israele woyna ñela sabbu kišona kudama laganda Kananata koluna fanaka iddi koke. Yesuwa kaba andabbu igin kokkkaragima Kananite suka kišama Yerikkota likida likide bare kisameke. Woyna sukowalle agodda Rahabda kokidama darka awanina kafaliyabbu kimin kiñama likida likide barowayesi laymunasi kidadiki abaye konakin miladinasi kiminima ndeya. Unu inna lilikida likidenaye kinadadimowa, Annasi kobinimasi deya. Inna likidalikidenamme Israele sukowa kobal kolana kolonala, Rahabsina itiya dinesina kufurunasi ñela sa mišoke.።

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-02.jpg?direct&}}

Israele ñelasabbu kišoma lagowatta kolunasi Yordan suba kokadandada. Yesuwasi Anna, “ekkena kaše košere anin gamunasi kimini”ske. Kaše košere subowatta immala, badda gatakin koma suba biyowa kobuce lakasso Israele laga alababbu kokadake.።

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-03.jpg?direct&}}

Agare Yordan suba kokadama kulasibbu guduratina kišama suka Yerikkotte aykedabbu kobacinissa Anna Yesuwasi kisasake. Agare Annasi fayyan kosoke. Masa abacacena kaše košerena Annam isasama kalla uyala mindellida uye kontallesi Yerikkosi sukowa higinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Israele sukowassi uya akontabarowa minde kontabare higin koleke. Komalda higimmala abacace awda awmmo kaše  fakara koleke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Yerikko suka kabe bušukun kosano Annam isasama kalla sukowalle gommesi ibal kinolake. Israeletab kišama Rahabna itiya dinokenasi kufurke. Kanana higidala gon koynama agara aññadiyedde Israele Yerikko koya kolama kotikama fanakala, imetana kode kobacinanda abbarenki okkayloke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-06.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israelesi Kananala kokosima agarowayette kosima damanabbu sulluma gomata komininnimasi kisasake. Iša ditta Kananite košerekin Gibyoneda kokkidama košere, Kananakin gerala kosima sukakin kolomende kodaki Yesuwasi omamajike. Israelette sulluma gomata gomatomunasi Yesuwasi kokkelake. Iša ditta Gibyone inkakin kolobessa Israelena Yesuwana Annasi okkelamme. Woynabbu Yesuwa Gibyonette sulluma gomata gomatoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-07.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Gibyone imamajima kotakemala anda kokošišinike. Iša ditta Anna kekala kokkamaši kolutamowano bayella kimminina šomumme. Fanake kulabbu Kananala gomma Amoraite lugusa, Gibiyone Israelette sulluma gomata gomatomma kitikke. Woynabbu samarabbu abacace kubuceki Gibiyonette masa sakaske. Gibyone dada koggošaki Yesuwata edetta kosameke.ኩ።

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesuwa abacaciye kubuce kikaki Gibiyone kidadinasi kisameno awada gera gadabbu karenki nawonke. Laga sarkasumala Amoraytesi lalasi kiboki iyake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-09.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna amelowa Anna Israelesi ibac kišoke. Amoraytesi Anna urfa kimanyena kiminki agegela uga kalle keleki agara arte kiya faske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-10.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israele fanaka udumala koyteki Amoraitesi koššo kibat kifalenasi uya kurasi kos kutama wagowassi kutuno lakaske. Woyna uyowalle Anna Israelesi bata fala anda kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-11.jpg?direct&}}

Anna abacacenaye okobat kokofalena kiminima kulabu, Kananite košera arte Israelette kobacinasi fen kokoweke. Iša ditta Yesuwana Israelena ibat kifalke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-12.jpg?direct&}}

Anna inna masena kulabbu ñela sabbu kišona kuda kišima lagowa košeriyeda ges kišoki, ide lagiyiddala sulluma kele kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-15-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yesuwa ito kišamala agariyesi summaski, Anna Sinay alyala Israelette ñelasa kokoso košimowana tillamunasi fayyamuna dada kišamowa kutuno okkayake. Agarowaye Annasi amanate kosa kosonasina fanediye goññemunasina ñela sa kowake.
