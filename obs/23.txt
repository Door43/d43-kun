====== 23. Yesu Košita ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-01.jpg?direct&}}

Mariya Yosefda kokidama kwa alalellette mikodigininasi sasala mikobin mišike. Yosef Mariya\\
katisuma kitikamala, dedowa ikka kišamma kišama itakke. Woynabbu Mariyasi oratos\\
kolanasi hesummeno widokabbu sakas kiwinasi lawaske. Woyna kiminimma ana Maliyaka\\
eyala konti kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-02.jpg?direct&}}

Maliyakowa akeske, “Yosef! Sasaleya Mariyasi nikanasi nokkaylome, ide kasiyala kinama\\
dedowa Aširma keddusakin kišamandeya. Deda adda nawsunaña. Yesu da kida kišo, (abbariya,\\
‘Anna ufuruma’) dandeya. Ayšisi agaresi mangeliyekin kufurunaña.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef Mariyasi itiyata kikano darkiya kišake. Iša ditta ašik deda kišinata unutte niñisumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-04.jpg?direct&}}

Mariya dagosa suda kidimmala, Roma lugusa kwe bubiya košitiya lagidata kodeki agare\\
kotada komininasi kokolattana faneske. Woynabbu Yosef kime Mariyatte Nazret sukakin\\
Betelihem sukata tabila gera ganki mimindadanno, ayšisi Betelihem imamala Dawit suka\\
kišamasi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-05.jpg?direct&}}

Betelihemta nawommala karemuna waga mitemme. Mitema wagowa šigide sudibidada\\
itandeya. Deda košimala mifalena aranta kosimmeno, iniña šigidesi woyn kišono oñgommala\\
kifalke; kidiya Yesuda mišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna awadowalle koriyala ajjala šigide lin kika gomma šigidade okos kolutake. Kolalasi leda\\
lesuma Maliyakam konti kišono ime okkayloke. Maliyakowa, “mikkaylome, tamma emesi\\
fala mayda naynakema, Andanda Messiya Betelikemla košike.” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-07.jpg?direct&}}

ganki dedowa hemu, unu sesabbu kofirfirtaki šigidesi ñada woyn kišomala kofalema\\
mitenamma.” ske. Woynokala kulya badda malikakebbu waske ime, “Šoda kulya baddala\\
Annasi kiša, lagala sulluma Annasi kosungudamesi kiša”nki Annasi kosaroke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-08.jpg?direct&}}

Šigidade etetiya fenki Yesu kofalema wagowatta ganke. Woynalle maliyakam isasamende\\
šigidesi weyn kišono oñamala kofalema deda koyteke. Oli kontimala anda gogonke. Batti\\
šigidadowaye šigidiye okosima wagiyata kode ganke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-09.jpg?direct&}}

Fanaka angude kulabbu sella konakin kwa šibabine onti koynamma šunda kontike. Inna\\
kweyane inna šundena Yude Lugusa košikeda alemata kišama kotakke. Woynabbu, lusowa\\
kontinasi okosima wagowakin tabila gera kokosodaki Betelihem sukata nawonke. Olle Yesuna\\
ašiyena okosima wagowatta kolike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-23-10.jpg?direct&}}

Kwa šibabinowaye Yesusi iniñatte kontimala gayn tun kokošodiki, Unusi aleba anda koynama\\
šodade kosoke. Batti itiyeta kode koliganke.
