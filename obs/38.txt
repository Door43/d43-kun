====== 38.Yesusi Abaye Konala Šan Kolama ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yude galli kelda šada furda kofurda koynake. Inna furdena galla arte anala, Anna imamalesi Eyitto saba goddakin aykedabbu kinola kikobessa woyna kokkayyanasi kofurda koynake. Yesu minda ekkenasi sasana samana kibinimakin galle satte badasi, Yesu imette kufurdanasina okodoka Yerusalemla koya famuna kišama olliyesi isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu olliyekin ella Yudada kokidamam kos kinake. Yuda apostolowayela šoldašula kibinima gos kinake. Iša ditta šoldasi anda kimema sabbatasi mindellela šulowakin šolda kunya kiñ kinake. Yesuna olliyena Yerusalemta nawmmala, Yude kobinna mareta kiki šolda kosobbu koniyala šas kola kišonasi gomatoske. Unu ina kiminimwa Yesu Messiya kišamowa kokkormemowana oya famuna kosan gommowana kitakemasi deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yude mare kaše andiya ika marosso Yudasi asseta šolde 30 kosoke. Yesusi šas kola kišonasi edesse anin edessomma kokala kišake. Yuda gomatoski šoldiyana kikki gaske. Yesusi kobinina otoma gas heske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yerusalemla, Yesu olliyette šada furda kufurdake. Šada furda amela ñada fanakala, Yesu añgera ginta naws kiteyki, “Yo inna keña. Inna emesi kosoma nyañamma, inna memin gommala kokayyañasi kemini.”ske. Inna akedabbu Yesu uliya imesi fitetos kišonowa kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Yesu nebita gibella nawski, “Inna keno, Koyšiša Tama kokobañamma inna mangele konenasi kuturuma, inna mino gommala dimala kokayyañasi kemini.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Yesu olliyesi, “emekin ella abasi bašaratosunaña”ske. Olliye awrowa kotikamala habenki, ‘akeda aykedabbu kišaññino, nano Okos kimininowa?’nki okkelake. Yesu, “Aba añgera facena nasona kwowa bašaratosuna”ski batti Yudasi angerowa kišoke

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda angerowa kibin kikama fanakowalle, Sadalla unula kuwake. Yuda Woynakin Yesu kobininasi kidadinasi Yude mareta gaske. Fanakowa awadandeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-08.jpg?direct&}}

Ñada kulabbu Yesuna olliyena lila alyata ganke. Yesu, “ tamma awada bubeye awiki gamunaña ‘Anadiya nayana garmiyena salan kosana’ suma kolattano koskeno.”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-09.jpg?direct&}}

Petro, “bubiye ewiyana aba okoda namininni!”ski ikas kišoke. Woynokala Yesu,“Sadalla eme bubeye kekana heske ditta Petro, kobinneya ibal kišannimasi dagan naso gonake. Iša ditta tamma awada dore bumunata minde satte Abasi nokkormeñña.”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-10.jpg?direct&}}

Okitowalle Petro Yesusi, “bala nabalena kišayyana enasi nakkormenni”ske. Olliya bodowayena okodoka kodake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-11.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin Yesu olliyette Getesemane suma wagata ganke. Yesu olliyesi darabala kolunnimasi dagamunasi kisasaki, woynakin iñalliya dagasunasi gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-12.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu minde satte akeski dagaske, “ O Abba, šos kišama kišayya inna hayda gibena abakin battada kola, ide inna hedaña išame hedeyanditta.”ske. Yesu anda šawsuma sabbatasi haykiya tiko tiko suma kokoba kokkaloke. Anna unusi kititona maliyaka kisame kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-13.jpg?direct&}}

Dagadella kimalama kelda olliyeta kideki koske. Iša ditta ime atode kolutake. Minda asatta kide kimala, Yesu , “ Targan Femu! abasi šas kola kišonowa iddi kokema.” Ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-14.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda, Yude kobinna marena, askarena, agara artettena koke. Gegadena gadaranena koynnake. Yuda Yesuta kiki, “Asasa, sulluma enasi kiša”ski šoñños kišoke. akes kiminimena Yude marowaye inka kobinini bessa alematandeya. Yesu, “ Yuda. Šoññoda šoññon kasokibe šan kawla nišona?”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-38-15.jpg?direct&}}

Askare oliki Yesusi kobinimala, Petro gegadiya kušeki kaše andiya harasi ukuna fatas faske. Yesu, “gegadeya wagiyata kikasi, darin kasonasi Maliyaka abacace kisame kasonasi awasi nakokelana šonana, ditta Awasi fayyanandada.”ske. batti Yesu kowa ukunasi ebaboke. Oliki Yesusi kobinimala olliye okotuda kolajike.

//Matteo 26:14-55, Marko 14:10-50, Luka 22:1-53; Yohanne 12:6, 18:1-11 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
