====== 46.Paulo Kristiyana Kišama ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-01.jpg}}

Stefano koya famma kwoye sese lis kišo kišima kowa Saulo deya. Yesusi kobinimme, woynabbu Yerusalemla kokosima akobinne kugura kinaki. Akobinna abišena darkena kibinki mabusa kutunasi itakin itata gas kinake. Kaše andiya Saulo Kristiyanesi Damaskokin kibin kililiki Yerusalemta kikas kikonasi yedasa kosono gas koske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-02.jpg}}
Saulo Damasko tabilala gas kosimala, kuya baddakin leleda anda lesuma higidiya bubiyala kibaña lesso, Saulo lagata koyake. Soulo, “Saulo! Saulo! ayšisi abasi agura gonno” suma mena kitikke. Saulo “Andaña ena nano?” ski kokelake. Yesu, “Aba Yesu. ena agura gonumowa deya!” akeski ikas kišoki.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-03.jpg}}
Saulo fesuma fanakala wa kintinasi šosumme. Kodiye konab kobinki Damaskota kokike. Saulo iñamma inomma uye satte goske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-04.jpg}}

Damaskola Ananiyada kokidama Yesu olliya koske. Anna Ananiyasi “Saulo kosima itowatta kaki, walida kitenasi koneya aniyala kidoro”ski isasake. Ditta Ananiya, “Andañña! Inna kwena akobinnesi aykes kugurabessa natikke”ske. Anna, “gada! Yudelana Anna faneda koynamma kwelana Aykes kiminki kidaña wasosunissa natawake. Ide kidaña sabbatasi tagamam arta kitena.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-05.jpg}}

Woynabbu Ananiya Saulota kiki koniya unula kidorki akeske, “Yesu, atta gan nišamala konti kesomowa, weye nikaski linunasina Aširma keddusabu wanunasina enata kasameke.” Saul woynokala wiye mikkateke. Ananiya udušeke batti Saulo ñadana kinki kalmina kišake




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-06.jpg}}

Saulo woynakala, “Yesu Anna kiša ndeya” ski damaskola kokosima Yudesi wasosuna kibinke. Yude, Yesu kokobinima Yudesi kibal kinolanasi darbes kinama kowa ununa tamma Yesusi kobinima kontimala okomalle kosake. Saulo  Yudesi Yesu Unu Messiya deyanda kuda kitalali kišoke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-07.jpg}}
Uya artoke kulabbu Yude Saulo koya famunasi komintike. Suka kaba daggela unusi tarbin koya famunasi kwe kisameke. Ditta Saulo gomatiyowa itikke, ide kodiye komanyenasi kodadike. Awadellala berentala kotuki sukowa kababbu tittin koleke. Saulo Damasko kin komanyemalana Yesu gogoda fala faloka kifaliki aniske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-08.jpg}}

Saulo Yesu olliyette kolenasi Yerusalemta kike, ditta ime kayla kokkayloke. Batti  Barnabada kokidama akobinnella Saulosi Apostoleta kikiki aykeski damaskola Yesu gogoda fala urfa iddirab samosubessa isasake.  Woyna badasi, Apostolowaye obin kokke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-09.jpg}}

Yerusalemla fesuma gurowabbu kolajima akobinnowaye Antokkiya sukata battan koliki ollena Yesuñña wasonke. Antokkiyala kokosima kwowayela artowaye Yudemme, ditta minda ekkenasi Akobinna arte kosake Barnabatte Saulotte maydabbu Yesuñña misasanasin košoda itowa itiribanasina otta ganke. Yesu kokobinimowaye mindiya Ekkenasi Antokiyala deya “Kristiyane” da kokkidama.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-46-10.jpg}}
Amelella Antokkiyala kokosima Kristiyanowaye okolugaki dagan kokosimala Aširma keddusa, “nakidama sanowa misananasi Barnaba kimme Saolotte kenatawa kaso.”ske. Woynabbu Antokiya Kristiyane Barnaba kimme Saulottesi koniye kodor dagan kišoke. Woyna kulabbu kwa košera aññadiyideta ganki Yesu gogogda fala samon misasano,  Kwa arte Yesusi kokobinke. 


//Appostole mina inta 8:3; 9:1-31; 11:9-12; 13:1-3 kin kokama Annawra fala.//
