====== 39.Yesusi Alalodasi Kokima. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-01.jpg?direct&}}

Tama woyna satowa awada farka kiddike. Kaše andiyowa kokelanasi askare Yesusi kaše andiyowa itata kokike.

Petro gera gerokalabbu kisake. Yesusi itowatta kotumala unu isalleb lakaski toma kini koske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-02.jpg?direct&}}

Itowa kasa kalab Yude mare Yesusi alalodasi sugun kokike. Iññabbu wasa ñera arte kikoke. Iša ditta,

awriyowaye awriye bile okoley kokayaki otummeno Yesusi Yude mare damana bubiyabbu fogadella koytena šomumme. Ina bubiyala Yesu ellana kudamme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-03.jpg?direct&}}

Komaldala kaše andiyowa wakeke liski, “inde asasaka, Ena Messiya Anna woyniyowa kišambe?”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “ye Aba deya, okodoka Annatte gonaki kulyakin nawon gonamana kantina”ske. Kaše andiyowa košišinabbu sesiya bereski kobinna marellowayeta “wasa bode hedimme! Anna kišandeya nsuma metikke. Nañki alalomuniñino tama?” ski arfaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yude marowaye bubiye kaše andiyowassi, “ibalena dada!” nki okas kosoke. Woyna kulabbu Yesusi gadibiya kofoki, jolabbu tifon kotaški, oyaki, wamiyoka obayike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-06.jpg?direct&}}

Petro laydokabbu itowa badalabbu kosimala hara kišella intiki, “enana Yesutte nišike!” ske. Petro awriyowassi kormeke.

Battina, kišella okodoka kokelake, tamana Petro kormeke. Komalda, agare, “Bareye Galileya dineno enana Yesutte nišama matakkenke”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin Petro, “inna mewda gomma kwena natakemme nañerama kišayya Annam atare”ski kamašike. Woynokala doram buske, Yesuna magiski Petrota liske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-08.jpg?direct&}}

Petro wagowakin išaki imba mema kimbike. Inna akes kiša gosumokala, šas kola kišoma Yuda, Yesusi Yude bala akkura kosoma kintike.

Yuda anda šawsuma sabbatasi ayniyabbu ja kolli koya faske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-09.jpg?direct&}}

Sella fanaka moliya Yude mare Yesusi okki Roma manna Platota kokike. Ime Plato Yesusi kona bayinaski alalos kolaki bala akkura kišona amanida kodorke.

Plato Yesusi, “Ena Yude Lugusambe?”ski kokela.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-10.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu ikas kiški, “ ayneyokana nudake. Iša ditta mannodaña, lagenaññanume, okoda kišakessa harañe obac kasona,

aba Annañña amana nafalinasi lagata nawke. Amana kimema bubiya abasi itik kasona”ske. Plato “ Amana ayšino?”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-11.jpg?direct&}}

Plato Yesutte mudama kulabbu iša koki, “inna Kwenakin fogadellana naytemma” ske. Ditta Yude marena agarena “isongolo!”nki awnke.

Plato, “no fogadellana kinamme.”ski ikas kišoke. Iša ditta andab alalanke. Plato minda asatta, “fogasuma inamme”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-12.jpg?direct&}}

Plato agarowaye bukunada kobonnimasi kayloki, askarowayesi osongolona kisasano Roma askare sesiya kowetiki doron koyaki luguse sesa tolosa biba koweke;

okodoka ota šaša ansangiyala kotu kosoke. Woyna kulabbu, “ Inne Yude Lugusa”nki obayike.

Matteo 26:57- 27:26; Marko 14:53-15:15; Luka 22:54-23:25; Yohanne 18:12-19:16 kin kokama Anna awra fala.
