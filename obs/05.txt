====== 5. Ñelasab Košima Dedowa ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-01.jpg?direct&}}

Abramatte Saraytte Kanana laga mimimakigalle šebe gonke,ditta ašik woyna okitowattana\\
deda minamme. Woynabbu Saray abišiyasi, “Anna deda našinasi kolano abana\\
darkišonamabbu, mharaña kišamanda Aggartte niñida unu udañab deda kiši kaso” ski udake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-02.jpg?direct&}}

Abramna Aggartte niñinke. Aggar deda adda nawsso Abram dedasi “Ismael”da kikidake.\\
Ismael galle šebnala satte kiddimala, Anna Abramta ide gunuske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abramsi, “Aba bubiya šonama Anna ndeya. Enatte ñelasa kakosona” ski isasake. Abram\\
gadibabbu lagata koyano Anna, “ena agara arte iwa nišana. Kanana laga enasina košereyesina\\
agitabbu nasona laga kišana, Aritata Anniya našana. woynabbu Enana iteyala kokosima adda\\
bubiyena kinito”ski isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-04.jpg?direct&}}

Anna, “darkeya Saray deda adda nawsuna unu ñelasa kinama kišana, kidiya Isakda nikidana;\\
unuttena ñelasa makosona, unu agara arta kišana okodoka Ismaelsina agara arta nawlana. Iša\\
ditta ñelañasowa Isak biloka kišana,”ske. Anna Abramsi, “Abraham” da kida kifegeda kolake,\\
abbariya “Arte iwwa” nda ndeya. Okodoka Saraysi “Sara” da kifegeda kišoke abbariya “Arte\\
niña” dandeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna amelowa itiyala kokosima adda bubiya kinitoke. Gallella kulabbu Abrham galle 100\\
sara galle 90 miminima fanakala deda addanawnke, Annam isasama kalla “Isak” da mikidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-06.jpg?direct&}}

Isak ito komala Anna Abrahamsi darabeski, “ ekka ellokanda Isaksi fiteta kalla kolaki fitetoda\\
kaso”ske. Abraham tamana fayyaski ikka kitenasi kossuke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-07.jpg?direct&}}

Abrahamtte Isaktte fiteta miminina wagowatta gan mikosimala, Isak iwwasi, “Abba, fitetasi\\
kišana uda inne kaynake, garmaki aykeno?” ski kokelake. Abraham, “Akka fitetasi kišana\\
garma Anna ayniyam ussu kasona” ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-08.jpg?direct&}}

Fiteta wagowatta mimimala Abraham ikka Isaksi kirabbu killiki Kanya baddasi kifalke.\\
Abraham ikka kitenasi andara kušeki koniya berkesumala Anna, “iwi, dedowassi bayellana\\
niminime. Abasi fayyanki ekka ellokowa nokogannimmiya kasoma natakke” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-09.jpg?direct&}}

Higidowalle Abraham garma buta giliya ela ñašela kuwama kintike. Inna Anna Isak udabbu\\
fitetasi kišana kussu kidoromandeya. Abrahamna gogos kišaki garmowabbu Annasi fiteta\\
kimin kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-05-10.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abrahamsi, “ninama bubiya kasonasi šadad šanki ekka ellokowasi kogannitta kasona\\
nimema kalla abana enasi našodina. Košereye kulya badda šundende fawmuna, ide fayan\\
kasomasi laga sasa agare enabbu šodine kosana” ske.

Nena fala 16-22. Kin kokkama Annawra fala.
