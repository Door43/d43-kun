====== 35.Baba Neda Yaynowa Fala ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-01.jpg}}

Amelella Yesu fuggara awawena okodoka otik kosonasi  humman koloma mangelina ellaydena kisasa koske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-02.jpg}}

Ide olle kolutama kobinna mara ellayde, Yesu mangelinesi iññata kikama kontimala, ayniye bilela awra kodana kobinke. Yesu woyna kintimala, Yesu inna falena kisasana kibinke. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-03.jpg}}

“Kwella dede bare kinnama kos kinake. Ikka damadowa iwasi, “Abba wo, maleyakin gedanda abasi agita kišanowa tamoka ged kaso!” ske. Woynabbu iwa ikkesi ges kišoke. "




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-04.jpg}}

“Batti woyna ikka damadowa anasiyoka inama bubiya kubuce kikki laga gerataki woyniyoski maliya bubiya mangelala koššeke.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-05.jpg}}


“Woyna kulabbu ikka damadowa gosumowa sokowalle lila andam feske. Dedowa ñadada kita kikana šolda dawske. Woynabbu makabesi ñada kisale kinajina sana kibinke. Anda dabes kišaki kolilima kolilowakin fesumabbu makabe ñadadowa kiñanasi tameske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-06.jpg}}
“Komalda, dedowa uliyasi, “ay namin gonano? Awa itala hare bubiye oñana ñadada koddima koynake, ditta aba kolila nakolili gonake. Woynabbu awata nade nayki harowayekin elle kawlanasi daganana”ske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-07.jpg}}

“Woynabbu dedowa iwa itata kidena kibinke. Unu attile  gerokalabbu kišamiya iwa intiki ide anda nes kiyake. Ajabbu kiki laws kibinki šoñños kišoke.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-08.jpg}}

“Ikkowa, ‘abba wo, Anna kekalana kekeyalana mangelonake. Ekka nakkidanasina hakanume’ske.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-09.jpg}}

“Ditta iwa hariyekin ellasi ikidaki, ‘itetala sesa faša kikoki akkasi kiwe! Koniyala magota, mindiyala asuga kusuge. Woyna kulabbu gogod kafurdanasi ašša ašada keko ketey, ayšisi, inna akkena abala kišike tamma woyniya kišake! Ibal kiša kišiki ikas koyteke!’ske.” 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-10.jpg}}

“Woyna kulabbu kwe bubiye gogon kofurdana kobinke. Anasiyoka ikka andowa isan kutama bišowakin ita koke. Abangalana anana menana kitikamala, ay kišaski komalleke.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-11.jpg}}
“Ikka andowa, iša damadowa yono gogon gomma kilatalalima kulabbu dagasowatta kuwanasina hesumme. Iwa yoki imette gogosunasi itata kuwaske ditta Anda košišinima sabbatasi ukuke”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-12.jpg}}

“Ikka andowa iwasi akeske, ‘inna gallena bubiyala amanatabbu nasana nasoke! Awreyakin ellokana lawn naynamme, woynasubbu išayyana kodañette gogon malutanasi afata kišellokana kaso ninamme. Ditta inna mangel minala bubiya koššema dedena ita komala, ašša ašada nite nišoke!’” 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-35-13.jpg}}

“Iwa, ‘akka, ena dima abatte nokoske, naynama bubiya eññandeya. Woynabbu gogon mafurdana haka, ayšisi, eyša abala kišike tamma woyniya kišake! Ibal kiša kišiki ikas koyteke!’ski ikas kišoke”


Luka 15:11-32 kin kokama Anna awra fala.
