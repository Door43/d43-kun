====== 1. Nena Fala ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg?direct&}}

Data bubiye osakolo kokkontina tulesuma damana Innakedabbu deya. Anna lagasina unula\\
kokosimesina uye kontallela kineneke. Anna laga kinnemala, laga gata kinamma šoma ifomana\\
ide awba gos kinake unula datellokana konnema kosimmme. Anna Aširma biyowa baddalab\\
solis kinake.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-02.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Anna faneski, “leda kiša”sso leles kišake. Anna intima leleda mayda kišano\\
“oddoma”nda kikidake. Leledowasi šomakin ketawaki šomowassi “awada“nda kikidake. Anna\\
nena uya ekkenala leleda kinneke



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg?direct&}}

Uya abarmala, Anna faneski laga baddalab kosima kuyowa kinneke. Unu biyasi waga\\
awbobbu biyaki kin ketawaki kuya kinneke.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Anna, “lagala elena ela kiša jinisena osakolo” ski fanesso okolatte kosa\\
koloke. Anna lisumala innemowa mayda kišama kintike.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg?direct&}}

Uya asallala, Anna awroka kudamab uya kurna terana šundena kineneke. Anna innayebbu uya\\
aganana awadana fanakena galla mintena gallena okkatawa kokotakena kiminke. Anna\\
lisumala innemowa mayda kišama kintike



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg?direct&}}

Uya akussumala, Anna awroka kudaki biya kasakala woyniyomunena baddabu fitti fittimuna\\
eta bubiyena kineneke. Anna innemowa mayda kišama kintike. Imesi išodike



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg?direct&}}

Uya akussumala, Anna awroka kudaki biya kasakala woyniyomunena baddabu fitti fittimuna eta bubiyena kineneke. Anna innemowa mayda kišama kintike. Imesi  išodike.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg?direct&}}

Uya akontallala Anna, “laga bubiyala sosona jinise bubiye osakolo” sso Anna udamende osakoloke. Imekin bode ita sosone, bode goggodab gade, ide bode ajja sososne. Anna  mayda kišama kintike.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg?direct&}}

Anna, “ gatiňňabuna kokaliňňabuna  kwe kanene, ime lagana sosone bubiye baddalana masulata koyna” ske.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu Anna lagakin lafuca kikki kwa kileyki, šuka hakas kutu kišoke. Inna kwena kida “Adum” da kokidake. Anna  Adum gosuna wagowalle ela kiše kutuke ide woynaye tabesunasi  kidorke.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg?direct&}}

Anna, “kwa iňalliya kišanasi maydamme”da kudake. Woyna sosonowayeki ellokana Adum\\
akodada kišana šosuma kos kinamme.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Adumsi atoda anda kele kišono atoske, atos kišamala dargiya mada kin sangella kikki\\
darka kileki Adumta kikoke.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg?direct&}}

Adum lis kintimala akeske, “inna aba kokkaloke, Abiša kin kiša komanno kidiya ‘darka’ nda\\
kiša” ske. Woynassi deya, abiša Iwana iniňana iwib darkiyatte elle mišana



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg?direct&}}

Anna gatiyabbu abišana darkana kineneki, “ekošeri fannammu lagasina wamu.”ski išodike.\\
Anna koniya sana bubiya mayda kišanama kintiki. Bubiyabu gogoske. Inna bubiya uye\\
kontallela kišake.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg?direct&}}

Anna uya akontabarala nena sana bubiya kimalke. Woynabbu isan kosima sanowa bubiyakin\\
hakes kikke. Woyna uya akontabarowasi išodiki keddusa kolake, inna kiminimowa\\
saniyakininna uyena hakes kikama sabbatasi deya. Anna kuyana lagana Immela kokosima data\\
bubiyena kinenemama innakedabbu deya.

Anna Awra fala: Nena fala1-2. Kin kokama.
