====== 44.Petrotte Yohannette Kwa Malyasi Mebaboma.. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Petro Yohannette fiteta itata gan mikoske. Fiteta ita udowassi middi mimimala dagada dagas kosima kwa malya mintike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-02.jpg?direct&}}

Petro malyowatta kitarbiki, “nasonasi šoldellana naynamme. Ditta naynamowa nasonaña. Yesu kidala feda komi gada!”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-03.jpg?direct&}}

Woynokala, Anna kwa malyowassi ebaboke. Malyowa solidabbuna habedabbuna Annasi kesaronasi kibinke, Fiteta ita dagasowalle kolutame anda kokomalleke

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-04.jpg?direct&}}

eboma kwowa kontinasi ansiyoka agare okowawike. Petro imesi akeske, “inna kwena ebomenabbu ayšisi mekomalleno? Ame kalmañabbuna mayda masamasinanume mababoma. Inna kwena ebomowa Yesu guduratabbuna Yesu išoma kobinnowabbuna deya” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-05.jpg?direct&}}

“Yesusi koya famunasi Roma mannasi mesasamowa eme deya. Šuka sasasi meyanke, ditta Anna obalemekin ikas kiboke. Memin mešimowa mebinimmansubbu išayyana Messiya tagama kitenowana ibalenowana koda košima edesse awrowaye tillasunasi Anna saneyenabbu nafos kikke. Woynabbu, mangeleye lushos kenolanasi tama Annata kede,.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-06.jpg?direct&}}

Fiteta ita marowaye Petro kime Yohannette mudama awrowabbu anda kokošišinike. Woynabbu imesi ibinki mabusa kutu kililike. Iša ditta agara artowaye Petro kime Yohannette mudama edettowabbu kokobinke. Yesusi kokobinima kwe kelediya 5,000 kalla kiddike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-07.jpg?direct&}}

Aberkenattabbu, Yude amarowaye Petro kime Yohannettesi Kaše andiyana, kobinna mara aññadetana kekata kikike. Petro kime Yohannettesi, “inna kwa malyena ayguduratibbu mebabono?”nki okkelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-08.jpg?direct&}}

Petro akes kikas kišoke, “inna kekeyala lakasuma kwena Messiya Yesu guduratabbu keboke. Eme Yesusi ton mesongoloke, ditta Anna woyniya kikas kiboke! Eme mebin mikammasubbu išayyana Yesu Kristo goduratabbu dutta kofura tabila ella kosimme.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-44-09.jpg?direct&}}

Marowaye Perto kime Yohannete iddirabbu mudano woyna habenke, inna kwename kwa ummiyoke ide kosasana minammeda kitakema sabbatasi. Iša ditta kwena kime woyna okitowalle Yesutte mišimowa kokkyake. Petrotte Yohannettesi haddada haddadomma kulabbu, sakan kiwino ganke.

//Apostole mina 3:1-4:22kin kokama Anna awra fala//
