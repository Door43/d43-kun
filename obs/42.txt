====== 42. Yesu Kuyata Kogotake ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu obalemekin kikas fesuma fanakowalle, Yesu badasase kin kwe bare Yesursalem sukowa korata gan mikoske. Tabilala mikosimala Yesula tillasuma damanowa mifali gan mikoske. Unu Messiya deyanki amanida kodor košike. Iša ditta oya fanke. Ide tama darkowaye woyniya koskenki odake. Imme inka mikobininissa mitakemme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu imeta seges kiki kotafeno ganke, ditta Unusi mitakemme. Yesu imesi ayšiññabbu midarma mulutabessa kokelake. Ime šamma uya angude ana Yesuññabbu malla ketuna date aykawle okominibessa woyna mifali mišoke. Ime kwa kelellaYerusalemla tillasuma damanenaññabbu kitakemmatte madarmi gomakeda lawanke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-03.jpg?direct&}}

Olle Yesu, Messiyaññabbu Anna awra nasubessa sas kifali kišoke. Messiya hayda anda kitenowana oya famunowana, iša ditta uya asattala woyniya fesunowa edesse oda košima kutuno mikkayyake. Kowamme karemuna lawamma wagowatta mimike, fanakowa laga  bagida kokalala deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-04.jpg?direct&}}

kwe barowame Yesusi imette karesunasi mikkelano, Unu yeske. Dirara ñadasi kolimala, angera faca nawski Annasi aftata kišoki, batti angerowa kiteke. Lalasi, Yesu kišama mitakke. Iša ditta woyna okitowa unu wiyyekin konyuma kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-05.jpg?direct&}}

Kwe barowame, “Yesu deya! Anna awra berkes kasasa gosumala urfiññe katokketti?” nki ayniye bilela mudake. Hadiriyokowassi Yerusalemta mideke. Mide mimimala Yesu olliyesi, “Yesu woyniyandeya, Mantike!” nki misasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-06.jpg?direct&}}

Olliye awroka koda kokosimala Yesu kolalabu agasiyala kolakaski, “sulluma emesi!”ske. ime aširma kofakke ditta Yasu, “Ayšisi mikkaylono, Ide tirimmo? Konañena mindañena kenti Aširme aba naynamende ula oynamme. Aširma kišammowa kitalali kišonasi ñadada kosob iñanasi kokelanao, isa šida kominti kosono inke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu akeske, “Aññabbu Annawrala kolatama bubiya tillassuññanda nasasa nawtake.” Woynokala Annawra kobininasi šibababiye woykes kišono Annwra kobininasi šonke. “Messiyya hayda kitenowana ibalenowana uya asattala obalemekin woyniya kikas fesunowana fanaka gerakin kolattake”da kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-08.jpg?direct&}}

“Okodoka kitabela kwe bubiye mangela konena koytenasi urfa kofegedasina ollañe fala maydena kofalinowa kolattake, ime Yerusalema kina kobinki falena kisasana, woynakin waga bubiyala kokosima košeretana agare bubiyetana gamuna, Innassi eme wase mikoske deya.”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-09.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Yesu ašik uye šebsalleta olliyesi minda arta konti kišoke. Tarbella agare muje kussume šamunesina giddatab konti kišoke! Unu woyniya kišama tabila artebbu olliyesi italali kišoke okodoka Anna mannodañña samos kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-10.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu olliyesi akes kisasake, “lagalana kulyalana elata bubiya abasi kasoke. Woynabbu gamu, lagala kokosima košere bubiyesi Iwana Ikkana Aširma Keddusana kidala kedušeki aba nasasamowa bubiya komininasi kesasaki ollañe kemini. Aba dima emette nakosima lamme”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-42-11.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu obalemekin kikas fesumasi uye šebsalle kulabbu olliyesi, “Aširma keddusa aneyala kobbu awa gudurata kesonata Yerusalemla lumu”da kisasake. Batti Yesu kuyata kogotake, dalama wiyekin kunyum kolake. Yesu neneda bubiyasi mannosunasi Anna tokonala gos goske.

//Matteo 28:16-20; Marka 16:12-20; Luka 24:13-53; Yohanne 20:19-23; Apostole mina 1:1-11 kokama Anna awra fala//
