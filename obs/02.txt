====== 2. Mangela Lagata Koma. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg?direct&}}

Adum darkiyatte Annam ussu kišoma jarjinowalle agogoda miša gon minake. Olle bariye\\
kurungiye gon minake išaditta lagala mangela kos kinammeno sarida kotik kiya kinamme.\\
Imme ela kiše wagowabbu solink Annatte midarmi minake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta darma ušurfinowa ela kiše wolle koski, darkowassi, “amana Anna ‘jarjinenalle kokosima\\
ela kišanayekin meňame sube?”ski kokelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg?direct&}}

Darkowa ikas kišoki akeske , “Anna bubiya ela kiša bubiyakin mañanasi kasasake tur,\\
maydana bayana ketub nitakena ela kišowakin ditta. ‘woyna elowakin meňame sanana\\
mesaneme, oynakin meňama kišaya mebalena’ ski asasake”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg?direct&}}

Darmowa ikaski, “inna kekanume. Bala mebalenni; ela kišowa meňayya weya katebu bayana\\
maydana metakki Anna kokala mešana woyana sabbatasi deya Anna meňame suma”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg?direct&}}

Darkowa lisumala woyna elakišowa, ňadasi mayda, wasi anda kilama, okodoka ašibaba kišana\\
heske, ela kišowakin kibaki iňke. Batti abaišiyasina kiki kišono ununa iňke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woynokala satowakin wiye mikkateno, kuringiye mišama kotik kiyano durfe mikaki sesa\\
kokalab donmikanasi fatatanke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna Adum kimme darkiyatte mikosimokala Anna ela kiše wagowalle solisuma mena\\
mitikki Annakin mikonyumake. Anna adumsi ikidaki, “ ikalle nokosso” ski kokelake. Adum\\
ikas kišoki, “ena ela kiše wagowalle solinuma mena natikki kurungaňa našamowano\\
nakkayloki nakonyuma gonake” ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg?direct&}}

Anna, “kurungeya nišama na esasano, niňame nama elowakin ikka niba niňabe”ske. Adum,\\
“inna abate kišanasi niko kasomena meňame numa ela kišowakin kiko kasono naňke” ske.\\
Anna darkowassi, “akeda ayniminno?” ski kokelake. Darkowa, “darmam amamajino\\
foganake” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg?direct&}}

Anna darmowassi akes, “ena bada kosina! kasabbu nokkoruku ganuna lafuca niňana, enatte\\
darkette abaye mešana, košereyana darkowa košerana abaye mišana, darkowakin košima\\
ansangeya sarkkosuna, košereya ochogoniyesi kinyina.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg?direct&}}

Anna darkowassi, “dagosala haydab nokodogosana, hedeya abišata kiša, unu baddeyala\\
mannosuna”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg?direct&}}

Anna abišasi, “darkeya awra nitikki abasi fayyanummiya aba niñame nama elowa kiša\\
niñamasi, laga memam ibinima kišake. Tama kofala andabbu ñadeya nibo niñana, batti\\
nibalena, uleyana lafucata kikas kidena.’’ Adum darkiyasi, agara bubiya niña kišana kišama\\
sabbatasi “Hawwa”da kida kišoke, Abbariya, “Šuka-sasa” dandeya. Anna Adum kime\\
Hawattesi šigida agalabbu kiweke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg?direct&}}

koñanasi yed kasonni” woynabbu Anna Adum kime Hawattesi ela kiše wagowakin battas\\
kinolake. Anna, ellokana woyniya elowakin kiñannimasi, Maliyaka guduratine tomina gegada\\
koyname ela kiše wagowa waydada udala kidorke
