====== 25. Sadalla Yesusi Mangela Kutub Iminina heski Darabes Kintima ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu kodušema kulabbu anasiyoka Aširma Keddusa badumma ererata kikike. Olle uye\\
šebsalle awade šebsalle luga goske. Woynakin Sadalla Yesuta koki mangela kimininasi darbes\\
kintike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg?direct&}}

Sadalla Yesusi, “Anna kiša nišamale kišayya niñanasi ugenassi angereta kifegeda!”ski darbes\\
kintike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “‘kwe woyniyomunasi ñadokanume okogošama Anna udakin kišama awra bubiyana\\
kogošana ditta’ nda Anna awrala kolattake”ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin Sadalla Fiteta ita tunkušata kigota kikiki, “Enale Anna kiša nišayya innakin košutte\\
duda, ayšisi, ‘mindeya ugala nokoyannimasi Anna maliyakiye kisamebbu elafona’nda\\
kolattakema”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-05.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta Yesu latta keddusela kolattama awrakin kikki Sadallasi kasa kišoke. Unu akeske,\\
“Anna awrala, ‘Anna Andeyasi daraben mentime’ski agariyesi feneske.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Sadalla laga mannodena ime oynama ankatiya bubiyana kogotiyana kišono\\
intino, “ abasi gayn nokošodi kasoyya inna bubiya nasona”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “ Sadalla abakin battada! Anna awrala agariyesi ‘Anna Andeyasi gayn keso Unokasi\\
gajomu’ ski faneske”ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu Sadalla darababbu kobat kofalemme. Woynabbu Sadalla iwi battaske. Batti maliyake\\
oloki Yesusi haron kosoke.

//Matteo 4:1-11; Marko 1:12-13; Luka 4:1-13 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
