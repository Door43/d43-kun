====== 27.Samarita Maydowa Fala ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Muse faneda ataka Yudella Yesusi darabes kintinasi, “Samina, Arita woyna godda\\
agitonasi ay namini?”ske. Yesu “Anna fanedala nas kolattano?”ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-02.jpg?direct&}}

Faneda atakowa Anna faneda, “ Anna andeyasi urfeya bubiyabbuna, šukeya udumalabbuna\\
gudurateya udumalabbuna laweya bubiyabbuna, kimey, išigoleyasina uleya kalla kimey suna”\\
ski ikas kišoke. Yesu, “keka nudake, inna kimini aritata woyniyonunamma” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-03.jpg?direct&}}

Faneda atakowa aba Alalada kitalalina heski, “nano išigolaña?”ski kokelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu falella kifaliki Faneda atakowassi kasiya kišoke. “Kwa ella Yerusalem kin Yerikkota gas\\
koske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-05.jpg?direct&}}

“Kwowa tabilala gas kosimala šifte oyteki inama bubiya kilin kokki osabbe koyaki maya\\
famake dabbu kowi ganke.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-06.jpg?direct&}}

“Woynakin arta lusumme, Yude kašella tabilowabbu gas kike. Inna kobinna amarena kilin\\
kokamana oya kowima kwowa kintimala dada kogošama kwenassi igošammiya kiwiki\\
tabilowa dogonatab kuski gaske.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-07.jpg?direct&}}

“Woyna kulabbu arta lusummiya Lewitellam ike. (Lewite Yude košere kosaki fiteta itala\\
haromune deya.) Lewitena woyna oyakin kilin kokama kwowassi intiki tabilowa kada ottab\\
kikadki igošammiya kus kiwi šaske.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-08.jpg?direct&}}

“Woyna badasi Inna tabilenabbu gaskoma kwella samaritam utake. (Samatire košera\\
aññadekinna Yudekinna kokodiginime deya. Samaritena Yudena anda kokkokkume gon\\
koynake.) Iša ditta Yudowa kintimala nedaya andabbu kintiki fulkuski šeyšiye killike.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-09.jpg?direct&}}

“Woyna kulabbu samaritowa kwowassi nawski, sandiyala ergaski kele wagata kikiki\\
fulkuske.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-10.jpg?direct&}}

“Sella fanaka, Samaritowa tabiliya anis gasundadanno, kele kaleda ita ayniyowassi šolda kiše\\
kišoki, ‘fulkuda, Innakin šolda nitaf nimalayya nade nawmala jabin nasonama’ske.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-27-11.jpg?direct&}}

Batti Yesu faneda atakowassi “ay lawanno? Maliya kilin kokki owi gammenassi kwe\\
sattenayela inkano išigoliya?”Ski kokelake. Faneda atakowa, “woyna mayda kimin\\
kišomowa”ski ikas kišoke.Yesu, “Gada enana unuyende kimini.”ski isasake.

//Luka 10:25-37 kin kokama Anna awra fala//
