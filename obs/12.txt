====== 12. Israele Eyittokin Kokkajarima ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-01.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Eyittokin kosamabbu anda gogonke. Woyna kulabbu innakeda sabenume. Ñelasa\\
kišoma lagowatta gan gonke! Eyitte Israelesi dahabana assettena aleba anda koynama šililena,\\
okokelama bubiya kišo kisameke. Eyittokin kosa gommala Israel dina kosamma košera elleyde\\
Annasi kokobinki Israelette kokotaf kokkajarike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna oddomala dalama tikeleydabbu aniya kinate gaski ika maroske; ide awadala toma\\
tikileydab kika maroske. Anna amelikelda imette goski kosodiya bubiyala kika maroske.\\
Ominina minam kosiyya Annokasi kosanokowa deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-03.jpg?direct&}}

Amela angude kulabbu Farawona agariyena, lawetiya kofegedaki Israelesi sabiye kokas\\
kinolana henke. Anna, Unoka Anna Amana,Anna woyniya, kosimowa agariye ontinasina,\\
Farawokinna anna ummiyekinna guduratina kišama kotakenasi Farawo urfa kilallabeke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu Farawo askariyette Israelesi sabiye kokas kinolanasi bada kutu kugurake. Israele\\
Eyitto askere badiyala koyšama kontimala, Farawo askarette Bahara bibatte agasala kokotingiri\\
kosama kobinke. Anda kokkayloma sabbatasi, “Eyittokin ayšisi kanola kakono? Tamma abal\\
kanolanaña”nki awnke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-05.jpg?direct&}}

Muse Israelesi akeske, “mekkaylome! tamma Anna efuruñña, ide emetab kiša kibac\\
kesoññamma”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Musesi Anna “agarañesi Bahara Biba kokadanasi kisasa.” ske,

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Anna dalama tikeleydowassi Israelette Eyyittette agasala kolano Eyitte\\
Israelesi kintinasi šomumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-08.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Musesi koniya Baharata berkeski Baharasi barela kifakenasi kisasake. Woyna kulabbu\\
Anna sawita kisameki Baharasi sergatana tokonatana doros kolaki tabila kola kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-09.jpg?direct&}}

Batti, biyowa sergallebuna tokonallebuna agichende lakasso Israel agare laga alababu\\
kokadake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-10.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu, Anna Israele Eyitto dinesi ibatama kontinasi dalama tikeleydowa oleys\\
kolake. Eyitto dine badiyasi kugurana kobinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-11.jpg?direct&}}

Baharata inki Israele kuguranasi kobinke, iša ditta Anna Eyitto dinesi tagama kišoki arabitiye\\
gada kikaylona kiminke. Batti akenki awnke, “elaji! Anna Israeletab kišaki ibac kišo\\
goskema.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-12.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Baharowa sullumab kokadama kulabbu, Anna Musesi Koniye Baharata kikas berkesuna\\
kisasake. Isasamende kiminimala, biyowa Eyitto askare baddala koki anis kutamende hilis\\
niñiske. Eyitto askare baharowalle kokošimfake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-13.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Eyitte okošimfa kocuma kontimala, Annasi amajonke ide Muse Anna edessa kišama\\
kokobinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-12-14.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Anna balakin kufurki saba goddakinna kinibišimasi anda kokomalle kosaki gogonke!\\
Tama Annasi haron kosonasi akkoybiše deya. Eyitto askarekin kufurumasina oytema\\
koybišowasina furda kofurdaki, Annasi agawabbuna asarabbuna Anana artabbuna konana\\
kosaroke.

//Kajara 12:33- 15:21 kin kokama Anna awra fala//
