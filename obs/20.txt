====== 20. Israelesi Saba Nawn Kika Gammana Okas Kodemana ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-01.jpg?direct&}}

Israel mannodala gomma agrena Yuda mannodala gomma agarena Annasi baganonke. Anna\\
Sinay alyala kišoma ñelasowa lawnke. Anna urfiye kokofegedaki mangeliye kokoneneki unusi\\
gajomunasi Edessa kisame kišoki isasake ditta, ime fayyamumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-02.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabu Anna mannode bariyenammela kokosima agaresi abayiye konala sakas kiwimab\\
akura kiyaki. Siriya mannodanda guduratina nedaya kinamma ide Iddira kišama lagowa Israel\\
mannodasi aškobon kolake. Siriye israyel mannodala gomma kwa arte kiya fañki, nafa\\
koynama data bubiyana koli kog gañki suka artena toma kutuke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-03.jpg?direct&}}

Siriye, amarena aburena konakošorka maydinena ummanki Siriyata nawn kika gaske. Iya\\
famumma šan kosama Adawdokowayesi kiwino Israel lagokala tarfonke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin Siriye Israel mannoda gos kišima wagowalle gomunasi kwe dibe kikoke. Kwe\\
dibowayebbu buškun kosama suke kikas kitake Israeleda tarfon košimowayesi idigin kikake.\\
Kwe dibe idiginima Israelowaye okošerimala Samariteda kokkidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda mannodala kokosima agare, Israel mannodala kokosima agaresi Anna kobinnittiyasina\\
fayyadittiyasina ikawli akuri kiyabessa ontike. Iša ditta attile woyna Kananite annena anna\\
ummiyena gajoda anin koganke. Anna abbaren kisasana edesse kisame kišoke ditta,\\
fayyamumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-06.jpg?direct&}}

Siriye Israel mannoda aškabon kiminima badasi galle mujella kulabbu, Yuda mannodette\\
kobacinasi Anna Bibilone lugusa Nebkudnassar kisameke. Bibilone mannoda guduraina gos\\
kinake. Yuda lugusa Bibilone Lugusa Nebkudnassar hara kišanasi gomatoski galla kay šolda arta fuggura artana jabis kinake \\
.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-07.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta galla angude kulabbu, Yuda Lugusa babilonesi baganoske. Woynabbu babilone ode\\
koloki Yuda mannodata masa sakanke. Yerusalem suka kobinki, olle kosima Fiteta itasi\\
bušukun kolaki fiteta italana sukala kosima šilila gitina bubiyana koli kokki ganke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda Lugusasi, baganodiya sabbatasi Nabkudnassar Aškare lugusa kiša addesi wiya kekala\\
kiya fanki, unusina wiya šunan kolake. Batti Babilota mabusala kokino itala kibalke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-09.jpg?direct&}}

Nabkudnassarna askariyena, Yuda mannodala gomma agare artowayesi Babilonta nawn kika\\
ganke tur kwa barowayesi biša kobonasi kiwiki ganke ditta. Anna agare, Anna ñela sabbu\\
kišoma lagowakin koša gamunasi kisbata kišake. Inna fanakena Saba kobina fanakanda\\
kokidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-10.jpg?direct&}}

Anna agariye mangeliya sabbatasi akuriya saba kokobinima kišayana, agariyasina išoma\\
ñeliya sasina lañasumme. Anna Agariyesi fulkudana Edessiyebbu awrana anidoka anis kikki\\
gaske. Unu galle 70 kulabbu ñelasabu kišoma lagowatta odena kišama amanida kišoke.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-11.jpg?direct&}}

Galle šebkontabare kulabbu Persia lugusa Sirus Bibilon mannodasi ibat kifalno Mannodowa\\
Persiya mannoda kišake. Israele kokidiya Yude da kišake imekin arte galliya udumala babilonla šan kolake. Yuda oto košama angudoke Yuda lagasi okkayyake.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-12.jpg?direct&}}

Persiya lugusa gangama ditta Inna agarenyela neda yina gos kinake. Sirusda kokidama Perišiye\\
Lugusa kišamakin artana lusummiya Yudata kodena hemma Yude bubiyesi edasi kišoke. Fiteta\\
itowa kode kotanasina mala kišoke! Woynabu, saba fanakakin galle 70 kulabbu, Yuda guja\\
angude Yerusalem Yudeyala kosima sukowatta kodeke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-13.jpg?direct&}}

Agare Yerusalemta kodemala Fiteta itowana Sukowa kaba agiccowana okas kotake. Inna\\
fanakenatta kwellebbu kokkanadi gomma kišayana, ñela sabbu kišoma lagowatta ode koli\\
woyniyonki Fiteta itowala gajonke.

//1Luguse 17, 24-25; 2 fanake kitabe 36; Ezra 1-10; Nehmiya 1-13. Kin kokama Annawra fala.//
