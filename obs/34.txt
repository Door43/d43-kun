====== 34.Yesu Fala Bodena Kisasama. ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-01.jpg}}

Yesu Anna mannodaññabbu fala arta kisasake. Kokalasi falella ella akes kisasake, “Anna mannoda kwellandem Sinapa* oja bišiyala kutumende deya! Unu oje bubiyekin daminkišowa deya.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-02.jpg}}
“Iša ditta Senapa oja  ito kišamala bišala kokosima ele bubiyekin anda kišana. Ašik šurke ñašiyela kosegedana hakketa kitona.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-03.jpg}}
Yesu fallelana kifalike, “Anna mannoda darkellam anja udumalasi hakkas kolanasi anja kurowa kutu nyakusuma kokala.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-04.jpg}}

“Okodoka Anna mannoda kwella konyumama bura biša kala kunyumama kokala. Kwa aññadella burowa kiteki, gogos kikšaki ikas kufuki inama bubiya kita kolaki bišowa kita kikama kokalandeya.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-05.jpg}}

“Okodoka Anna mannoda aleba anda kinama killoba dada kokalandeya. Dade atarebella dadowa kitemala, inama bubiya dabos kolaki dadowa kita kikama kokala ndeya.”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-06.jpg}}


Woyna kulabbu Yesu miniya maydasi kokkasaroki kwe bode kibayima kwe fala kifalike. Akeske, “kwe bare dagada mimininasi fiteta ita mimike. Ellowa fuggura awawa, ellowa kobinna mara.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-07.jpg}}
“kobinna marowa akes košodike, ‘O Anna, aba kwa elle kokala tafura dinana, askaminana, awana aminana, okodoka inna fuggara awawena kokalana našammowassi aftata nasoke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-08.jpg}}
“kokalasi, aba sambatala minde bare luga nakolugana, naytema šoldakinna šililekinna ašeba nawlana.’




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-09.jpg}}


“fuggara awawowa kobinna marowakin battas lakaski, wiyyena baddata galasumme. Okodakin sogasi koyaki, ‘O Anna, aba mangelinamma nanyumoda kanti’da kuda dagaske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-34-10.jpg}}
Batti Yesu akeske, “Amanabbu nasasake. Anna fuggara awawowa dagadasi itikki alalanda kudake.  Ditta Kobinna marowa dagadasi Anna imemme. Anna uliya kokogotamesi inalena, okkale kosamesi igotana.”


//Matteo 13:31-33,44-46; Marko 4:30-32; Luka 13:18-21; 18:9-14  kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
