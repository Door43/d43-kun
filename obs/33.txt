====== 33. Bišababowa Fala ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Yesu daba udala summamma agare kisasa koske. Agara arta fawsum udumalab lakas kisasana waga kitenasi daba udala kutama aunkwala tigiske.  Aunkwowalle aguski agarowassi isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu inna falena kifali kišoke, “biša ababella elega išidasi fes kišake. Išisumala elegowaye magadde tabila dummo, šurke bubiye kofe koñameke”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-03.jpg?direct&}}

“Elle lafuca angudoka koletana arta kinama lagala dunke. Talla fawsuma wagala dumma elegenaye etetoka kokolatte hawn koloke ditta lagakata boba kosamenasi šosumme. Uya daññosumala mekon kokolab kosake.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-04.jpg?direct&}}

“Attile elega bode tara kala dunke. Hawn  koloke ditta tarowa išimfama sabbatasi inna  tare agasala hawn kolomenaye oja kotunasi šomumme.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-05.jpg?direct&}}

“Elega magade laga totomala dunke. Hawn koloki bode 30 elle 60 okodoka bode 100 košike. Ukuna kinamowa itika!”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-06.jpg?direct&}}

Inna falena Yesu olliyesi artiñgida kutuke. Woynabbu akeski berkes kifali kišoke. “Elegowa Anna awra deya. Tabilowa Anna awrasi itikama ditta awrowassi ibinimma, ide sadalla awrowa tafuras kuful kikama kwa ndeya.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-07.jpg?direct&}}

“Talla kinamowa, Anna awrowassi gogodabu kibin kikama kwan ditta tagamana gurana yomala kobiniya fas kiwina.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-08.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna tarina lagowa, Anna awra itikama kwandi ttainna lagena lawana burana sudiya sabbu Annala kinama mowa fas kiwiki ikka išimmiya fas lakasuma.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-33-09.jpg?direct&}}

Laga totomowa Anna awarasi itikki kobinki, oja mayda kišima kwa”.

//Matteo 13:1-8, 18-23; Marko 4:1-8, 13-20; Luka 8:4-15 kin kokama Anna awra fala//
