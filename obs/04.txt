====== 4. Anna Abrahamsi Ňelasa Kišoma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-01.jpg?direct&}}

Akura biya šasumasibbu galla arte kulabbu kwe arta kokošeri fannnanke. Bibiye ňelelloka\\
kodana koynake. Anna faneda kišomende laga sasata salamuna owiki samarabbu sukella\\
kodorke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-02.jpg?direct&}}

Ime akkasarena Anna udama awrasi fayyaditte gon koynake. Kulyata agun kolina henki\\
baddata kodora kotana kobinke. Inna kwenaye baya lawinena miniya baya tafoka kotafema\\
kosama Annaintike. Mangela anada baya komininaasina šonke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-03.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu anna Andiňňa Awriye burkes kinolake. Agarowayesi laga sasata salas kinolake.\\
Woyna ota kokosima sukowa kida Babel dandeya. Abbariya, “košumbure kišama”da ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-04.jpg?direct&}}

Galla muje kulabbu Anna Abramda kokidama kellasi gunuski, “lageyana ašeyena kiwiki aba\\
nasob nintina lagata gada, olle enasi našodiki, agara artana nawlana ide kideya kotak kišama\\
nawlaki ešodimesi našodina okodoka kosa ketaremesi kosa natarena, enabbu lagenalle\\
kokosima kwe bubiye našodina” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-05.jpg?direct&}}

Abram Annam udamasi fayyaski darkiya Sarayna hariye bubiyena datiya bubiyana kikaki\\
Anna nasob nintiñña suma kanana lagata gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-06.jpg?direct&}}

Abram kanana lagata kimala Anna, “igideyala kosima lagena bubiya kitarbi, ninti gonuma\\
lagena bubiya enasina košereiyesina agitab nasoññamma”ske. Batti Abram lagowalle\\
woniyoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-07.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Abram Melkezedekda kokidama Anna baddišindowa kaša anda kiteke. Melkezedek\\
Abramsi akes išodike, “Anna kulyana lagana kinnema badda Šindowa Abram kišodi.”\\
Abramna Melkezedek si inama itidiyakin Ašeba kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-08.jpg?direct&}}

Galla artoke šanke iša ditta Abramtte Saraytte deda minamme. Anna Abramsi ikaski amanida\\
kišoke, deda adda kišinowana košeriye kulya badda šundende fawmuna kišamowana kisasake.\\
Abramna Annam išoma ňela sowassi kobinke. Abram Annam išoma ňelasowassi kobinno,\\
kobiniyasi “alala” nda kokela kišoke.{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-04-09.jpg?direct&}}

እግዚአብሔርምከአብራም ጋር ቃልኪዳን አደረገ። ቃልኪዳን በሁለት ወገኖች መካከል የሚደረግ ስምምነት ነው። እግዚአብሔርም አብራምን“ከገዛ አብራክህ ልጅእሰጥሃለሁ” አለው።“ለዘሮችህም የከነዓንን ምድር እሰጣለሁ።” አብራምግን በዚያን ጊዜ ያለ ልጅ ነበር።
