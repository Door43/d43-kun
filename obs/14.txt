====== 14. Badummala Selenamma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-01.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israelette kokosoma ñela sowa magadeda fanedowaye tillamunasi kisasama kulabbu, Sinay alyakin ganke. Anna agariyesi Sinay alyakin Kananada kokidama sukanda ñelasab nasona suma lagowatta kika maroske. Dalama tikeleydowa Kananata kinateno agare badiyasi ganke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna ñela sabbu nasonansuma lagowa Abrahamsina, Isaksina, Yakobsina, košeriyesina agitabbu kišonowa amanida kišo kišike. Iša ditta woyna fanakowa lagowalle agara aññadiyidda arte gon koynake. Ime Kananite dabbu kokkidake. Kananite Annasi fayyan kosomme ide gajon kokošodimme. anna akoñeresi gajonki mina anda bayana komin koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israelesi, “Ñela sabbu nasonannama lagowalle Kananitesi ebal kenola, Imette sulluma meminime, mekodiginime. Olle kokosima gajodiya anne ebal kenola. Abasi fayyamuna mekuyya, abasi gajomuna uda anniyowayesi gajomuna”ski isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-04.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Kanana laga hirowatta segen kolimala, Muse Israel košere šebanala barowayekin kwellidda abiše 12 kinolake. Inatawama kwowaye, lagowa aykawli bessana olle kokosima Kananowaye waye kalminebe kalmittebessa likin kontinasi kisasaki isameke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-05.jpg?direct&}}

Inna kwe šeb anala barenaye Kanana laga uye šebsallesi solin kontiki odeke. Ime akenke, “lagowa totoma kinina udumala kinake” nki agarowayesi osasake. ditta Lilikida likida šebowaye, “sukowa suka borka, olle kokosima kwena sukkasukka gere! imette kabacimale kišayya abata kafalena ide aya kanolana.”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-06.jpg?direct&}}

Likida likidowayekin barowame,Yesuwatte Kalebtte, “Amana Kananite gerena sukka sukka guduratine, okodesubu ošayyana kime imesi kabata kafalena. Ayšisi udiññab kibac kasona Anna kišama sabbatasi.”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-07.jpg?direct&}}

Inna fanakenalle agare Kalebtte Yesuwattesi fayyan kišomme. Muse kime Aronteta kode kokošisiniki, “Inna laga bayenatta ayšiññi kakono? Masala mabalebbu akkena darkañena saba kolumakin Eyittola goddam mayda”nke. Agare Eyittota kika kidena amara kotawa kokana henke.።

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-08.jpg?direct&}}

Anna anda košišiniki Unutte Kola Tendowatta koke. Anna, “Eme Abasi baganonmma sabbatasi inna agarenaye badummenalle selenamuna; Yesuwa kime Kalebtte ditta galle šebbare okodoka šebbarekin baddata koyname bubiye badummenalle kobalena; ditta ñela sabbu nasona nama lagowatta olunni”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-09.jpg?direct&}}

Agare inna kotikamala ominima mangelowassi tasnanke. Masiya sulube kikaki Kananitette kobacinasi ganke. Iša ditta Anna imette kosimmeno, Muse masata gammeski kolake ditta otik kosomme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-10.jpg?direct&}}

Masowatta Anna imette gasummeno okobat kokofalke. Kwe arte okoyake, woyna badasi Israele kananakin odeki galle šebsallesi badummala selenanke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-11.jpg?direct&}}

Israel agare badummala selenamma fanakowalle, Anna Israele tabeys kinake oñanasi kulya baddakin “Manna”da kokidama ñadada kele kišoke. Nya koñanasi wagiyata Wiwite kisame kišoke. Anna woyna fanakowalle okowema kowediya kišano asugiya ašosumma gosuma kimin kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-12.jpg?direct&}}

Anna faga minabbu tallakin biya faffas kišono onoke. Inna bubiya kimin kišomiyana Israel agare Musetana Annatana kodeki garonki ñurunke. Iša ditta Anna Abrahamsina Isaksina Yakobsina kišoma ñela sowa tillasunasi, amanatiyokala luske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-13.jpg?direct&}}

Agare biya šillay dawmma fanakala, Anna Musesi, “Tallowa biya faffasunasi kuda faffasunañamma”ske. Iša ditta Muse tallowassi awra kudana udabbu gujabbu minde bare kiyake; innakes kiminimabbu agarowaye agasala Annasi ankata išomme. Kwa bubiya inona biya tallowakin faffaske. Iša ditta Anna Muse minowabbu košišiki Musesi, “ñelasa lagowatta ena nuwanni”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-14.jpg?direct&}}

Israele galle šebsallesi badummala selenammala, Annasi baganomowaye bubiye obal kokomalke. Batti Anna agariyesi ñela sabbu kišona kudama lagowa korata kika kike. Woyna fanakowalle Muse ito kišake; woynabbu Anna agare marosuna kidadinasi Yesuwa ketawa kišoke. Anna Musesi, išama fanakela Museyende marosuna edessella kibo kišonowa ñelasa kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-14-15.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi ñela sabbu kišona lagowa kintinasi, Anna Musesi alya tunkušata agus kinasi kisasake. Muse ñela sabbu kišona kudama lagowassi intike, lagowatta kuwanasi Anna edasa išomme. Woyna kulabbu Muse ibalke. Israele uye šebsattesi amadon kosoke, Badiyasi amara Yesuwa išake. Yesuwa Annasi kobinki ide fayyasuma amara fadaba ndeya.

//Kajara 16-17, solida 10-14, 20, 27, kosada 34 kin kokama Anna awra fala Kajara//
