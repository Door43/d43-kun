====== 32. Yesu Aširma Bayam Ibinimatte Darka Badinatte Kinababoma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-01.jpg?direct&}}

Mindella Yesuna olliyena aunkabbu baharowa komintiki Geresene dine okosima wagata kolike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-02.jpg?direct&}}

Baharowa kokadana koddi kolimala Kwella aširma baye obinimella Yesuta keji kike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-03.jpg?direct&}}

Inna kwena kwa ellokana goññesuna šosumma kwa guduratina ndeya. Kwe mindiyena koniyena lolebbu kobin kollimana lole kite kinola kinake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-04.jpg?direct&}}

Kwowa woyniyodiya nabule ajjala gos kinake. Awadana uyana awda aws kinake, sesena kowenni okodoka ugabbu minda arte uliyasi kofak kinake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-05.jpg?direct&}}

kwowa Yesuta komala kekiyala koyaki gays košodike. Yesu woyna aširma bayowassi, “kwenakin iša!”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna aširme kinama kwowa mena andabu, “Yesu, Anna baddišinda Kiša, abakin ay henno? Tori nakema hayda asome?”ski awske. Yesu aširma bayowassi, “kideya nano?”ski kokelake. Unu “ame arte masama sabbatasi kidaña Ligion dan deya.”ski ikas kišoke. (“ligion” ella Roma askarela alufe arte kibinima gujan deya.)

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna aširma bayowaye, “tori makema inna higidenakin gerata asameme.”nki daganke. Koriyowalle alya fafala makabe guje okonadi kokosso, Ime, “tori makema makabenayeta malunasi kasamenke”nke. Yesu, “gamu”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-08.jpg?direct&}}

Aširma bayowaye kwowakin osaki makabowayeta koluke. Makabowaye kofetowallebbu kokošutteki baharowatta dun ganke. Makabowaye kelediya 2,000 osana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-09.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna makabe kinadi gommowaye išama bubiya kontimala sukowatta kolaji koliki, Yesu iminimiwa bubiya kodake. Sukowa dine oliki aširma baya kina kišima kwowa kontike. Unu ebo kišaki sesiye koweki widabu kwa luše kalloka gos kosima kontike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-10.jpg?direct&}}

Olima kwowaye anda kokkayloma sabbatasi Yesusi wagokin iwi gasunasi daganke. Yesu aunkata aguski gasunasi kossumala, aširma baye kinna kišima kwowa, “enatte ganana Yesu?”ski edasa kokelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-11.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Yesu, “okoda išanni. Iteyata kide kaki Anna aykimin kesobessana inkes nanyumos kiminibessana ašeyesina kodeyesina nisasana henake.”Ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-12.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu gaski Yesu imin kišoma bubiya kwa bubiyasi kifali kišoke. Faliya kotikame bubiye fakkarabbuna komallabbuna wan kosake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu baharowa kadata kideke. Olle kimala agara arte igin koleki ošimfa šimimilanke. Woyna agarowaye agasala galle šeb-anala-baresi kokoba ajam ikayloma darkam utake. Ebonasi šoldiya bubiya sarenadala jabis kimalke, attile baydoka bays kiyake ditta kale kišomme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-14.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, inikoma arte kinababoma fala kitik kinaki, “Yesu sesoka nasaneyyana abana nabona.”ski lawake. Woyna sabbatasi Yesu badallebbu kiki sesiyasi kisaneke. Sanoka kisanemette woynokala kokobiya aja komintike!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-15.jpg?direct&}}

Woynokala Yesu uliyakin gudurata išama itakke. Magiski, “na asanno?”ski kokelake. Olliye, “higin keleki doron gomma kwe artoke okosima, ‘na asonno?’ da ayšisi nokkelano?”nki okkelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-16.jpg?direct&}}

Batti darkowa katodabbuna kaylabbuna Yesu kekala koyaki, aykes kiminibessana ebo kišabessana woynokala kisasake. Batti Yesu, “kobinneya efurkema sullumabbu gada.”ske.

//Matteo 8:28-34; 9:20-22; Marko 5: 1-20; 5:24-34; Luka 8:26-39, 42-48 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
