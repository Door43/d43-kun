====== 8. Anna Yosefsina Iwa Ita Dinesina Lilakin Kufuruma. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob adikišos kišamala, amellala imema ikka Yosefsi šigide badala kokosima osamiyowaye\\
ayken kokosibessa intinasi kisameke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yosefsi Iwwa Yakob ikka bubiyekin šada kime kinake, okodoka Yosefna ine baddala\\
mannosuna kišama eyabu kintima sabbatasi osamiye anda koku koynake. Yosef, ine ki\\
kintinasi gaske, imeta kimala ine obinki sabe kotarebima kwela kota kolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef osame itiyata kodemma ana Yosef kowe kišima sesowassi berin kolaki sesa (lašša)\\
kokobala šifinen kolake, woyna kokobala šifenamma sesowa iwwasi koki kosono intike. Unu\\
Ajja sosona adame oya fankeda lawaske ide anda amadoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-04.jpg?direct&}}

Sabe kotaribima atarebowaye Yosefsi Eyittota kogganke. Eyitto Nilo suba serala kosima suka\\
andana guduratinana deya. Sabe kotarebima atarebowaye Yosefsi hakumatala masulata kinama\\
kwa aburellala kota kolake. Yosef andiyowassi mayda haros kišo kinake, Annana unusi\\
išodike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef andiya darka, Yosef kikki niñisuna darabeske. Ditta Anna kekala Anna kekala mangela\\
namininni ski henamme ske. Darkowa košišiniki ñerabbu bašaratoski mabusa kibin kutuke.\\
Ditta mabusa ita kulumasubu išayyana Annala amajosuma sabbatasi Anna ollena kišodike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef kwa koniya bayasubu koytemma išayyana galle bare mabusa itokala goske. Eyitto dine\\
lugusa, Farawo, artingida kiša kiyama eya kaye bare kayyeke. Gomata sasiyeki ellokana\\
eyowaye kinifirina šosuma kosimme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-07.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Yosefsi Eya kayya kifirina šosuma sa kišoke. Woynabbu Farawo Yesefsi olaki iññata\\
kikike. Yosef eyowa kifir kišoki akeske, “Anna galle kontabare bura galle kisameñña, badiyasi\\
galle kontabare lila galle košañña”.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-08.jpg?direct&}}

Farawo Yosefbbu Anda gogoski, Eyitto laga bubiya badiyasi abarmiya kiša mannosunasi\\
kigotake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-09.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef agaresi bura galle kontabarenayela kina arta koki kowawina kisasake. Batti lila gale\\
kotabarowaye olomala Yesef šuwwowayekin kinowa kolakie agareta kitake okodabbu\\
koddima ñada koyteke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-10.jpg?direct&}}

Eyitto bilokala dittiya Yakob itiya dinette gon koynama Kanana sukowallena lila anda kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-11.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob ikkesi kina kota kokonasi Eyittota kisameke. Yosef osame kina kota kokanasi koliki\\
Yosef kekala lakanke, ditta osamiya Yosef kišama otakemme. Yosef mayda kitakke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-12.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef osamiyesi adabiya ašinkowa kin okofegedama kitakenasi darbes kintima kulabbu\\
akeske, “mekkaylome aba osameya Yosef deya saba nasanasi katakawlamala akkeda baya\\
lawan kasoke, ditta Anna bayowassi maydata kifegedake. Eyittola kaweki gomu, iteya dinesina\\
meggošama data bubiya nassu nasoñña” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef osame sukiyata kode koliki Yosef šukab kosima kosasamala Yakob anda gogoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-14.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob gallabbu anda kito kišama kišayana itiya dine bubiye kikano Eyittola gonke. Yayob\\
ibalemma ana ikke bubiyesi kišodike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-08-15.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abrahamsi kišoma ñelasowa Isakta šas kolaki, batti Yakobta, okodoka Yakob kiše\\
šebnala barowayetana itiya dine tana šaske. Yakob Kiše šebnala barowaye košere Israel košera\\
kule šebnala barowaye kosake
