====== 16. Akkoybiša Kinolana Marowaye ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesuwa ibalema kulabbu Isaraele Annasi fayyan kosomme, tarafon košima Kananite košeresina lagowakin ugura kinolamme. Okodoka Anna amanowa udabbu Israele kanane anna ummiya gajoda kobinke. Israele lugusa oynamme, woyna sabbatasi kwale lawetiyidda imesi keka kokkalo kiyama tillanki okosoda koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-02.jpg?direct&}}

Israele Annasi fayyan kosona kokuma sabbatasi Anna abaye konala sakas kiwimabbu akura kiyake. Abaye Israele date bubiye kuful kikaki, bišiyesi ambon kinolaki imekin artesi amuska minabbu kiyake. Israele Annasi fayyamumma sabbatasi abayiye galla artesi mimabbu kika gommo, Israele abaye konakin kufulunasi urfiya kokaski Annata daganke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-03.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Anna abaye konakin akkoybiša kinolana mara kibokišono akkoybiše kikinolaki, lagowalle sulluma kišoke. Woyna kulabbu Annasi lankosaki lakilema anna ummiyesi gajonke. Anna inna kintimala, išigoliya abayiyeda Midianesi kibono obaciki Israelesi ibata kifalke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-04.jpg?direct&}}

Midiane galle kontabaresi Israele bišela kišima kuful kikake. Israele anda kokkayloke; Midiyane intinasinnimasi dadela laynke. Komalda tagis kiyamala Israele ufurunasi Annata awnke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-05.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Gidionda Israelella Midiyane kiniya kokannimasi laydabbu kina kijiñ koske. Anna maliyakam iki, “Anna enatte, abacaca iddira. Feda gadaki Israelesi Midiyane konakin ufuru”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-06.jpg?direct&}}

Gidiyon iwa lakilema annesi fiteta kimin kišoma kanya kina kinake. Anna Gidionsi inna kanyena bušukus kolanasi kisasake. Ditta  Gidion agarowayesi kayloma sabbatasi ašik bagisunata luske. Woyna kulabbu kanyowa bušukus taws kolake. Woyna anna ummiye fiteta kanya kišimowa korasi Annasi kišana fiteta kanya tama kewaki olle Annasi fiteta kimin kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-07.jpg?direct&}}

Abarkenatabbu molala fiteta kanyowa tuñgus kišama otakki anda kokošišinike. Gideon koya famunasi itiya kolino, iwa, “ ayšiññi anneyasi medadinasi daraben gommo? anna kišayya ayniyabbu daris kikannibe”ske. Inna kudama sabbatasi agarowaye Gidionsi oya famumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-08.jpg?direct&}}

Batti Midiyane tamana Israelekin kuful kokansi kokas koloke. Kelediyabbu fommankin kelana nikelannime. Gidion Israele ellabbu Midiyanette kobacinasi kikidake. Anna unubbu nafos kikki Israele kufuruna kišama kitakenasi alemate bare kišobbu intinasi Annasi dagaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-09.jpg?direct&}}

  - Alemata ekkenowa, garma agalasi lagala kidorki molala isgosuma uba agalowalle ditta lagala kalennimasi dagaske. Annana dagadiya kalla kimin kišoke. Abarkenatabbu sudala Gideon Annasi  isuma ubowa lagokowalle ditta garma agalowassi isanemma kiwinasi dagaske. Wamiya  Anna dagasuma kalla kimin kišoke. Inna alemata barenaye Gidionsi Anna unubbu nafos kikki Israelesi Midiyane konakin kufurunowa mitalalina miša mišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-10.jpg?direct&}}

Israel abacace 32,000 Gidionta kolike, ditta Anna abacacenaye arta fawmma kisasake. Woynabbu  Gidion masala  akkayle kosama abacace 22,000 itiyata kisameke. Anna Gidionsi tamana abacacenaye keladiya arta fawmma kisasake. Woynabbu  Gidion kwe 300 kikaki, tarafommowayesi itiyata kisameke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-11.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna awadowalle Anna Gidionsi, “nokkaylonnima Midiyan abacace kambata idaka awriya nitikayya.”ske. Woynabbu Gidion Midiyan abacace kambowatta iskiki, Midiyane abacacella kodiyasi eyabbu kintima data kisasa koske. Kowa kodiya akes kisasake, “ inna eyena abbara Gidion abacace Midiyan abacacesi kibata kifaleñña!”ske. Gidion inna kitikama Annasi gajoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-12.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Gidion abacaciyeta kide kiki fakarana salana šibila tomana kišoke. Midiyane kambiyala atoda kokosimokala sudubasi higin kileke. Gidion abacace šibila tomowassi sala kala kotumasi toma leleda kontimmeno Midiyana abacace 300 šibila tomowa leleda otimme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-13.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Gidion abacace tarballebbu saliye tawn kinolano šibiliya toma bubiye iñña tarbellabbu les kontike. Fakariye kileki, “gegaja Annaññana Gidionñana.”nki awnke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-14.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Midiyane abacace ušumbure kinolano, ayniya bilebbu kokoya famuna kobinki. Woynokala, itiyeddala kokos kolutama Israele okkidaki okodadiki Midiyanesi uguraki iyano kwe are ocuke Tarafommayesina Israel lagakin han kinolake. Uyowa Midiyane 120,000 ocuke. Innakeddabbu Anna Israelesi ufurke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-15.jpg?direct&}}

Agarowaye Gidion lugusiya kišana henke. Gidiyon woyna komininasi edasa išomme, Iša ditta Midiyanekin kokama dahaba magotowaye kokinasi kokelano agarowaye dahabana arta koki kosoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-16.jpg?direct&}}

Gidion dahabowassi ikiki kaše andiye sesa kokkaloma sesa aññada kileyno, agare gajoda anna ummiyiya kokala gajomuna kobinke. Inna sabababbu Anna Israelsi akkura kiyaki abaye konala sakas kiwike. Komaldala agare ufurunasi Annata awmmo, Anna ime kufuruna mara kwella kibo kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-17.jpg?direct&}}

Innakes kinnema minda arta kosanke: Israkyele mangela ominina, Anna akura kiyana, Ide kodeki urfiyeb tasnammala Anna ide kufurunasi mara kibo kišona. Galla artesi Anna Israelsi abayiyekin Akkoybiša kolana mara kibo kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-16-18.jpg?direct&}}

Komaldala, agarowaye Igidiyala kokosima agarowayende lugusa kibo kišonasi Annasi daganke. Ime lugusa gera sukkusukka iddira okodoka, masata kika marosuna henke. Anna kokeliyowassi hesumme, iša ditta fayyamuna kokuno Lugusa kidor kišoke.
