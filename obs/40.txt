====== 40.Yesusi ton kosongoloma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-01.jpg?direct&}}

Askarowa Yesusi oda kobayima kurabbu ton kosongolonasi kogganke. Osongolona fatakada elowana nawsuna kominke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-02.jpg?direct&}}

Askarowaye Yesusi, “Golgota” (abbariya anasanga aw ani giba) suma wagata kokiki, koniyen mindiyena fatakada elala tonke. Ditta Yesu, “O Abba, ominimena otakemmema samanada kišo” ske. Pilato, “Yude lugusa” suma latta kinama alemata Yesu ansaga baddowalleb kolatta kodoronasi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg?direct&}}

Askare Yesu sesa kokanasi murka fanake. Inna komin gommala, “sesañasi murka fadab gen kokke” suma eddessa awrowa tillaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesusi šifte bare agasala tona kosongoloke. Ellowa Yesusi baya awra kudake. Ditta ellowa, “ena Annasi nokkaylonnibe Kimme koniñña fogada, ditta Inna kwena fogada inamme’ ske. Batti Yesusi , “toro nakema mannodeyala abasi kaya” ske. Yesuna, “tamma paradisola abate nokosina” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yude marena agarowaye agasala kokosima kwa elleyde Yesusi baya kobayi kodake. “Anna kiša nišayya fatakada elenakin idaki uleya kufuru! Woyna badasi makobinininama” nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woynalle laga oroba uya farka kiddimala, lagowa bubiya išoma kišake. Uya farkakin ašik sisida sate sateta išomamiya luske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Yesu, “koddi tillaske! O Abba, aširmañana šukaña koneyala nasoke” ski golgaske. Woynokala asangiya gaysso šukiya išake. Ibalema fanakala laga iškida anda išgiske. Fiteta itala kosimanda agaresi Annakin kinatawa kinama Hoynada sesa andowa daddaki šuttiyata bertis kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu baliyabbu kwe Annata kolona tabila woykes kišoke. Woyna Yesusi digon kokosima askarowayekin ella inna kišano intimala, “šadiya inna kwena fogada inamme, amana Anna kišandeya” ske

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg?direct&}}

Batti Yesu Messiya kišama mikobinima Yude Amare bare, Yoseftte Nikedemotte, Yesu ula mikanasi pilatosi daganke. Uliyowassi mikiki sesab mifirfitaki, tallala koyya kodadima nabulala miki mutuke. Nabulowa uda uga andikattab mufufura miki kabunke.

Matteyo27:27-61; Marko15:16-47, Luka 23:26-56; Yohanne19:17-42 kin kokama Annawra fala.
