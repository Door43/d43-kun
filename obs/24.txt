====== 24. Yohanne Yesusi Kudušema. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yehanneda Zekariya kime Elsabet yette kišowa ito kišaki Edessa kišake. Badumma ererata\\
kigoski, ajja golana galawana kiñki kowediya Arkuba kimabbu kawama koweke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-02.jpg?direct&}}

Agara arte Yohannesi kotik kosonasi badummata kosa kolike. Imesi unu, “Anna mannoda idde\\
kokema urfeyabbu tasnanki kede.”ski samos kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yohanne edettena kotikamala, arte mangeliye oratommo, Okodoka Yohanne udušeke. Kobinna\\
mare artena Yohannebbu kokodušenasi koloke ditta urfa okofegedamme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yohanne kobinna marowayesi, “eme darma bajoliname, urfa kekofegedaki mineye kefegeda.\\
Oja mayda kišimma ela bubiya tamata famuna. Yohanne edesse deyam, “lida, tabileya kussuna\\
asamedaña aneya nasamena”nki odamowa deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda ellayde oliki Yohannesi Messiyabe dabbu kokkelake. Yohanne akes kikas kišoke, “ aba\\
Messiyamme, ditta badañala kona kwellam koske, Unu illiyabbu anda ndeya; aba asugiya taba\\
nafirinasina nokoskamama”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-06.jpg?direct&}}

Uya aberkenala Yesu Yohannebbu kodušenasi koke. Yohanne intimala, “limu, woyna laga\\
mangela woleys kolana Anna garma”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yohanne Yesusi, “ aba enasi nadušenasi haka naynama kwanume woynakin ena abasi kaduše\\
ditta”ske. Ditta Yesu, “ kominina dada šadiya kišama sabbatasi ena abasi kadušena dada”ske.\\
Woynabbu Yesu mangelansubbu inamma kišayyana Yohannebbu kodušeke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu kodušema kulabbu biyakin agusumala, Anna Aširma uba kokkalomab kontiki Unula\\
kaleke. Woyna fanakowalle Anna mena kulyakin, “namayma Akka ena deya. Enabbu anda sus\\
kasoke”ski udake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-09.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Yohannesi, “Aširma keddussa nuduše nokosima kwekin ellala kalebbu nintina, woyna\\
kwowa Unu Anna Kišandeya.”ski isasa kutake. Anna elloka koske. Ditta Yohanne Yesu\\
kudušema fanakowalle, Anna Iwa gunusuma kitikke. Anna Ikkana kintike Unu Yesu deya.\\
Okodoka Anna-Aširma Keddusana kintike.
