====== 9. Anna Musesi Kikidama ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yosef ibalema badasi duguliye bubiye Eyittola lunke. Ime galla artesi olloka gon kokošerike.\\
Ime Israeleda kokkidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-02.jpg?direct&}}

Galle muje kulabbu, Israele arta kokošeri fannanke. Eyitte Yosefsina miniya mayda bubiyasina\\
lañan kosake. Israele arta fannammma sabbatasi Eyitte kayla kobinke. Fanakowalle Eyittola mannos kinama Farawo Israele Eyitto sabe kosana kiminke.\\
{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-03.jpg?direct&}}

Israel dine ita andena suke jamara udumalena kitanasi Eyitte kisabonke. Sana artabbu tagama\\
anda koyteke, ide Anna išodino deda arte košike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-04.jpg?direct&}}

Farawo, Israele deda mirka arte kišima kintimala, agariya, Eyittesi, okošima deda mirka adda\\
bubiya Nilo subata fan kutuna faneda kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-05.jpg?direct&}}

Israel dina darkella deda adda nawske, ununa abišiyatte šomma kallabbu dedowa\\
munyumanasi darbenke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-06.jpg?direct&}}

Ašiye dedanasi munyumana šomumma mitakema fanakala, dedowassi yafadakin mufurunasi\\
biyata nisunnima sessegakin kosanama jambila mutuki Nilo suba makalala šike agasala\\
midorke. Misama sola dedowassi ay kišanissa intinasi kotul koske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-07.jpg?direct&}}

Farawo kiša kiša subowatta kimala janbilakintiki kasiya kala ay kosibessa okoski kintike. Deda\\
kintimala ikka kalla kikke. Farawo kiša kišowa, dedowa tabesunasi Israel dina darkella\\
kikoke, ikoma darkowa dedowa niña kišama itakemme ditta. Dedowa ito kišano iniña awsa\\
kotimala, dedasi farawo kišata kikas kiki kišono kida “Muse” da kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-08.jpg?direct&}}

Muse kwa anda kišama fanakala, amelella Eyitte kwella Israela sabellasi kiya kosima kintike.\\
Muse Israela kodiyowa kufuruna darbeske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-09.jpg?direct&}}

Kwellana intimma abareski Eyitto dinowassi iya faski, iki kabbaraske. Iša ditta Muse\\
iminimena kwellam inti kutake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-10.jpg?direct&}}

Muse iminima minowa Farawo itikamala, iya fasuna heske. Muse Farawo askarekin uliya\\
kufurunasi badummata kotuda kejike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-11.jpg?direct&}}

Eyitto kin gerala kosima badummala šigidada kišake. Ima sukowalle darka kidiginki dede\\
adde bare kišike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-12.jpg?direct&}}

Amellela Muse šigide kinadi kosimala, toma bibbisuma šarbata kintike. Ditta tomowa\\
šarbatowassi iñamme. Muse koñama šarbatowassi korala kikitarbinasi seges kike. Toma\\
bibbisuma šarbatowatta seges kimala, “Muse lakan gonuma wagena keddusamma asugeye\\
koweti” suma Anna mena kitikke.\\
{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-13.jpg?direct&}}

Anna, “agarañela isuma tagama nantima sabbatasi Farawota nasamebbu, Israelesi Eyitto saba\\
goddakin ninolañña. Abrahamsina Isaksina Yakobsina nasonasi ñelasa nasoma Kanana laga\\
nasona”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-14.jpg?direct&}}

Muse, “agarowaye asamemowa kotakenasi kokkelama kišaya nanaki nakas nasoñino?”ske.\\
Anna akeske, “Aba, Aboka ndeya. Woynabbu ‘Aboka’ emeta kasamekedaki isasa. Okodoka\\
‘aba Anna woyniya, emamala Abrham, Isakna Yakobna Anna. Inna aritata kita kokidaña.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-09-15.jpg?direct&}}

Muse kayla kayloma sabatasi Farawota gasunasi hesumme, imala awra mayda itak kudanede\\
inne kiyammeno. Woynabbu Anna Mišama Aronsi unutte kisameke. Muse kimme Arontesi\\
Farawo Israelesi sakan nawinniski urfiya kolallabena kišamana anis kisasake.

Kajara 1-4 kin kokama Anna awra fala.// //
