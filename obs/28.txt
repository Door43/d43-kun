====== 28. Tamfeda Aburowa ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-01.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella tamfeda aburella Yesuta kiki, “Asasa mayda Arita woyniyoda naytenasi\\
aynaminindadino?”ski kokelake. Yesu, “ Ayšisi “Mayda”da akidano? Mayda ellokam koske,\\
ide Unu Anna ndeya. Iša ditta ena Arita woyniyoda nitena henuya Anna faneda tillada.”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-02.jpg?direct&}}

Tamfeda aburowa, “tillanana Fanedenaye aykayeno?”ski itaf kokelake. Yesu, “kwa niya\\
fanume, awanonume, nunyame, niñerame, ewasina eneñasina añkatoda kišo, ide išigoleyasi\\
uleya kalla kime sumowaye deya”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-03.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta tamfeda aburowa, “ inna fanedenaye damadañakin naddi tillanake. Woynabbu attile\\
Aritata woyniyonanasi aynamini?” ske. Yesu lis kintiki imeke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “udumala nišanasi ninama bura bubiya kita kolaki adawdesi kišo kulya baddala maleya\\
ninanamma. Woyna kulabbu badaña kasa.” ski ikas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-05.jpg?direct&}}

Tamfedowa Yesu udamowa kitikamala, unu anda aburanno inamowa kišona hesummeno\\
šawski Yesusi bada kišo kide gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-06.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna fanakowalle Yesu olliyesi, “Abure Anna mannoda kowanasi anda taggima! Ye, Abura\\
Anna mannoda kuwamakin arkubam nibira boralabb kuwa šasumam hayokoma”ski isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-07.jpg?direct&}}

Olliye Yesu udano otikamala šawnki “akedabbu na šos kofuru?” nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu olliyowayesi itarbiki, “inna kwabbu šos kišanni, Annabbu data bubiya šos kišana”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-09.jpg?direct&}}

Petro Yesusi, “Ame bubiya mawiki badeya masake. Mayataña aykiša?”ski kokela.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-28-10.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “abasiddabbu iteyana, osameya addena kišena, iwana, ineñana, aw ikkena kiwimowa,\\
minda 100 kibattamana, arita godda kitena. Iša ditta anitane arte kulitane kosabbu kulitane arte\\
anitane kosana”ski ikas kišoke.

Matteo 19:16-30; Marko 10:17-31; Luka 18:18-30 kin kokama Anna awra fala
