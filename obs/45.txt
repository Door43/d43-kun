====== 45.Stefanotte Filippotte Fala ======


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-01.jpg}}

Akobina ekkenowaye maron koynama marowayekin ella Stefanoda okidama kos kinake. Una kokida mayda kinama aširma keddusabuna šibababbuna wasuma kwa gos kinake. Stefano faga mina kimininke, okodoka kwe Yesusi kokobininasi kekab berkes kifali kišo kinama kwa gos kinake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-02.jpg}}

Amelella Yesu fala kifali kišo kosimala, Yesusi kokobinimma yude Stefanotte šara koboke. Anda kokošišiniki Stefanoññabu kobiniya maresi, “Musesina Annasina  baya kudano matikke!” nki ñerabbu šakenanke. Woynabbu Yude  marowaye  Stefanosi obinki kaše andiyatana Yuda amara aññadiyetana kokiki, Stefanoññabbu wasa ñera aññadena kika segemuna kominke. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-03.jpg}}

Kaše andiyowa Stefanosi, “inna kodamena amanambe”ski kokelake. Stefano Abrahama kin ašik Yesu fanakata Annam imin kišima mina ande bubiyena okodoka Anna agare daymindabbu Annasi fayadittiyaññana kutuno okkayyamabbu kasa kišoke. Batti akeske, “eme sera alabena amengelena mišama agarame, eme šadiyanda emamale Annasi baganonki edessesina kiya fammende, dima kwe Aširma keddusasi baganomuna, iša ditta eme imekin šasuma baya miminke! Messiyasi meya fanke.”ske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-04.jpg}}


Yude kobinna marowaye Inna kotikama fanakala, ukuniye kokotomaki meniye kogota awnki anda kokošišinike. Stefanosi sukaki išata goggon kokiki oya famunasi uge fanki oyake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-05.jpg}}

Stefano ibalenala meniya kigotaki golgas awski, “O Yesu, šukaña kibin kika”ske. Woynakin kokitiyebbu tuski, “Andaña, inna mangelena nikela nišome” akeski  udama kulabbu ibalke.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-06.jpg}}
Sauloda kokidama kwella Stefano koya famma kwowayette kosamaraki sesiye lis kišo koske. Woyna uyowalle Yerusalemla kokosima kwa arte Yesusi kokobinimesi kugura kobinno, akobinne waga aññadiyideta kolajike. Kogurowa kosimana, ime olima waga bubiyala Yesuñña samon koynake. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-07.jpg}}

Filippoda kokidama Yesu badisasella Yerusalemla kogura fesumala tefan kosama akobinowayekin ella kos kinake. Unu samariya kiki Yesuñña samosso agara arte okofurke. Amelella Anna kin kosamema maliyakella Filipposi badummata gasuma tabilata gasunasi kisasake. Filippo tabilowabbu gas kosimala, Itoppiya dina hakumatala masulata kinama arabiyala aguski gas kosima kintike. Filipposi Anna Aširma otta kiki kwowatte kifalinasi kisaasake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-08.jpg}}

Filippo arabiyowatta sugus kimala Itoppiye kwowa eddessa Esaya ilattama lattakin magadella kikida koske. Kwowa akesuma kikidake, “Ta kotena garmende kogganke, garman wisumende, miyakin awrellokana gunusumme. Hakitta kominke ide ankata osomme. Šukiyasi unukin ola fanke”




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-09.jpg}}

Filippo Etioppiya dinowassi, “nikida gonumena nibinibe” ski kokelake. Itoppiya dinowa, “Ayya. Asasab  nabinina kosimmab, nabininasi šonanni. Awa korañala godaki Esaya ilattamena uliyaññambe kwellaññambessa asasa” ske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-10.jpg}}


 Filippo Itoppiya kwowassi Esaya Yesuññab kilattamowa sas ifali kišoke. Ide okodoka  Filippo Yesuññabb kodama gogoda fala Annawrla kolattama bodenkinna kikoki sas kifali kišoke. 




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-11.jpg}}
 Filippotte Itoppiya kowayette gan mikosimokala biya kinama wagata mimike. Itoppiya Kwowa, “Lida! Woyne biya koskema! Koduša nakanasi šonana?” ske. Filippo “kristo yesusi nokobiniya šonuna” sso. Batti Arabiya gurowassi lakasunasi kisasake.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-12.jpg}}
Batti biyowatta in mimino, Filippo Itoppiye kowassi udušeke. Biyowakin agumma kulabbu Aširma keddusa woynokala Filipposi naws kikki wagelata kikino, Yesu gogoda fala kwellelana anis kiggaske.




{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-45-13.jpg}}

Itippiye kowa Yesusi kitekemasi gogos kišaki, lagiyata kide gaske.

Appostole mina 6:8-8:5; 8:26-40 kin kokama Annawra fala.
