====== 10. Akure Šebowaye ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-01.jpg?direct&}}

Musette Arontte Farawotamimiki Akenke, “Israel Anna Akeskeske, ‘agarañesi sakadab\\
gamu!’”. Ditta Farawo itik kišomme. Israel dinesi akkoybiše sakasuna udabbu osan\\
košimowakinna sanna taggima kosanana kisiboske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-02.jpg?direct&}}

Farawo agarowayesi nawib gamunnisumiya kišake. Anna Eyittosi akure taggime šebe kele\\
kišoke. Ide inna akurenayebbu Anna farawo kinna Eyittola kokosima anna ummiye kinna\\
guduratina kišama Farawosi išono intike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Nilo biyasi kokobata kifegedake, iša ditta tamana farawo Israelesi sakasub gamuna\\
hesumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-04.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Eyitto bubiyata ñoñe kisameke. Farawo ñoñowaye oles kinolanasi Musesi naloske. Ditta\\
ñoñe bubiye ocu kosama kulabbu Farawoa urfiya kolallabeki Israelesi Eyittokin sakasso\\
gamumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu Anna ñañe kisameke. Ikaski antane kisameke. Woyna kulabbu Farawo Musesina\\
Aronsina Inna akurenaye mutub lakamuya Israelesi Eyittokin sakasub gamunowa kisasake.\\
Muse Annata dagasso antanowaye Eyittokin oleys kinolake. Tamana Farawo urfiya kolallabeki\\
Israelesi sakanab gamunniske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-06.jpg?direct&}}

Ikaski Anna Eyitte šigide bada taggimabbu kocuna kiminke. Farawo urfa kolallabeki Israelesi\\
sakas kiwib gamuna hesumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulassibu Anna Musesi Farawo kekala hayna išigilasunasi kisasake. Muse haynowa\\
kuya baddata iškilasumala Eyitte ulala tangulleda šayse sugunke, ditta Israelela tanguleda\\
šayše okkontimme. Anna Farawo urfasi Ilallabeno, Farawo Israelesi sakasso gamumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-08.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi, Anna Eyittola dirgisuma biša waga bubiyana okodoka isata kosa kolime kwena\\
agegelabbu kiya kinolake. Farawo Muse kimme Aronte kikida Kikiki, “Aba mangela naminke;\\
tama gamunasi šomuna” ski isasake. Woynabu Muse Annata dagasso Agegelowa saynas\\
kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-09.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta Farawo ikaski mangela kiminki urfiya kollalabeke. Israelesi akkoybiše kinola kisameb\\
gamunasi hesumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-10.jpg?direct&}}

Woyanabbu Anna Eyittota uule kisameno agegelam iya kigambema biša dirga bubiya\\
koñamenke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg?direct&}}

Ikaski Anna uye sattesi lusuma šoma kisameke. Šomowa akkeda anda Eyitte itiyakin kosanana\\
šomumma kišomake. Israele woyniyomma wagowa leledokanditta.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-12.jpg?direct&}}

Inna Akura eldawdenayebbuna, Farawo Israele sakasub gamuna hesumme. Farawo fayyasuna\\
kukumasi Anna komalda akura ella kitaf kišonasi kimintike. Inna Farawo urfa kifegedana\\
šosuma akura ndeya.

Kajara 5-10 kin kokama Annawra fala kajara.
