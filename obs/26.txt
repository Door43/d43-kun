====== 26. Yesu Harodiya Kibinima ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu Sadalla darabe kibat kifalema kulabbu, Aširma Keddusa guduratabu waski Unu\\
išimowatta Galileyata higidanda kide kike. Yesu wagakin wagata gaski isasake. Kwa bubiya\\
maydodiyaññabbu koda koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu damadiyala gos kinama Nazretta kike. Sambata amela yude summada wagata kike.\\
Unusi Edessa Esaya ilattama, firfitabbu kodoroma agalala kolattama, latta kikidanasi nawn\\
kosoke. Yesu agalowa berkeski olle kolattamowakin magadella agarowayesi kikida kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, “Anna Aširma abala koske, woynabbu baresi fala mayda, okkollimesi koybiša, ummalesi\\
wa lida, okomassomesi ibiša fanenana. Okodoka inna Andiñña ayiba galla kišama nasasanasi\\
kasameke” ski ikidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu ikida kimalki igoske. Agare bubiye Unokata kotarbike. Woyna ikidama Anna awra\\
magadowa Messiyañña kušura kudama kišama otakke. Yesu, “inna nakida nasoma awrena\\
tama tillaske”ske. Agare bubiye okomalleki, “inna Yosef kišowanumebe?”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu olle akeske, “edessellokana sukiya dinebu ankata itennida kodamowa amana ndeya.\\
Edessa Eliya fanakala Israella darka sola kose wade okos koynake. Iša ditta Israella gallesatte\\
sarasi lilam fesuma fanakowalle Eliyasi Anna Israel sola kosabbu kosalenasi isamemmeki laga\\
aññade sola kosata kisameke.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu itafki awriya anis kiggaske, “Edessa Elisa fanakala Israella agala bada koyname arte\\
ošike, ditta Elisa imekin ellokasina ebabomme. Israel abayela mara kišama Naman bilokasi\\
agala badakin kebaboke.” Yesusi fayyan kosono ifali kišo kosimowaye Yudeno, woyna\\
kotikamala unuta anda kodam kokošišinike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-07.jpg?direct&}}

Nazret agare Yesusi summada wagowakin goggon kolaki torallebbu kofetanasi fofogata\\
kokike. Ditta Yesu agare agasokowabbu kimintiki Nazretta kide gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu Galileya higida bubiyala soliske. Agara arte kika koloke, ontina šomummena, gamuna\\
šomummena, otikana šomummena, awra kodana šomummena ika kinikoma arte Yesuta kika\\
kolono inababoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-09.jpg?direct&}}

Aširma baya kasiyakala koynama kwa artena Unuta kika kolike. Yesu faneda kišono\\
aširmowaye kwewayekin osake. Minda artoka, “Ena Anna kišandeya!”nda koda awn koynake.\\
Agare fakkaronki Annasina gajonke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-26-10.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin Yesu apostoleda kikidama kwe šebnalabare kinatawake. Apostolowaye Unutte\\
ganki Unukin kokosasake.

//Matteo 4:12-25; Marko 1:14-15, 35-39; 3:13-21; Luka 4: 14-30, 38-44 kin kokama Anna awra Fala//
