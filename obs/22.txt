keddusabbu wasuna, agaresi Messiya kobin kokanasi kususuna” ske. Zekariya, “Abatte\\

Maliyakowa Zekkariyasi, “Inna nako nasoma gogoda falena Annam udama. Abasi\\
amajonumma sabbatasi ašik dedowa košinata Awra šon nitak nudanni” ski ikas kišoke.\\
Woynokala zekariya maytoma kišake. Woyna kulabbu maliyakowa Zekariyasi iwi gasso,\\
zekariyana itiyata kike darkiyana katiske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-04.jpg?direct&}}

Elsabet katisumasi tere kontalle kinama fanakala, Maliyakowa Mariyada kokidama Elsabet\\
kebesiyellata ki konti kišoke. Unu abiše kitakemma Yosef da kokidama kwellette\\
mikodigininasi sasala mikobinke. Maliyakowa, “ katinuñña adda nawnuñña Yesuda kida\\
nikidana, Anna baddi šinda kišanda kokidana ide aritasi mannosuñña” ski isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-05.jpg?direct&}}

Mariya, “Aba abiše natakemma inna aykawlab kiša?” ski kokelake. Maliyakowa, “Aširma\\
keddusa enata koñña, badda gudurata hellis kesoñña. Woynabbu dedowa keddusa Anna kiša\\
ndeya” ski berkes kifali kišoke. Mariya kobinki maliyakowa udamowassi ibin kikke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-06.jpg?direct&}}

Maliyaka uda kimalama kulabbu Mariya Elsabet kikikidanasi kike. Elsabet Mariya išodimowa\\
kitikamala kasiyala kosima dedowa bartakas habeske. Imme Annam imin kišomowabbu\\
mukusunguda gogonke. Mariya Elsabet yette tele satte goski itiya kideke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-07.jpg?direct&}}

šoda kite! Ena akka, agaresi mangeliye samanada ikawlab koyte kokanibessa nudana Anna\\
baddišinda edessanda nokkidana.” ske
