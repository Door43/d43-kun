====== 49. Anna Asoma Ñelasa Tama ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-01.jpg?direct&}}

Maliyakella Mariya da kokidama solellasi, Anna Kiša kišina kišama kisasake. Woynabbu unu Attile sola abišena kitakemmiya Aširma Keddusam hellis kišono katiski, deda adda nawski Yesuda kikidake. Woynabbu Yesu Annana Kwana deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu Anna kišama kotalalima faga mina arta kiminke. Biya baddala komi gaske, biya arantasi šaññasso wis kišake, Badina arte kinababoke, aširma bayena kinolake, obalemesi balakinna kiboke, okodoka añgere kussumena Isa damade barebbuna agara 5,000 baddala kosana agaresi kididinasi kiminke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-03.jpg?direct&}}Okodoka Yesu asasa Anda, Anna kiša kišama helatabbu kudake. Uleyasi nimemema kalla kwa bodesina nimendada kišama kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-04.jpg?direct&}}

Okodoka Unu data bodesi nimenankin baddala Annasi nimendada ndabbu kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, Anna mannoda, lagala kosima aleba anda koyname bubiyakin šasumandeya”ske. Kosima kwasi nafa anda kinama Anna mannoda dina kišanowa deya. Anna mannodata melunasi mangeleyekin mekofurunadada kuda kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-06.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu kwa ellayde okobinki okofurunowana elleyde okobininnimowana kisasake. kwa ellayde laga maydende, Yesu gogoda fala kokobinimowaye okofur kosana. Ellayde Anna awra uwammana aykišayyana oja kišinnima koletanina lagende deya. Innaye Yesuñña kisasama edettowa kobin kokammowayena, mannodowatta kolunnime deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu, Anna mangelinesi anda kimemowa kisasake. Samanas kiwiki ikke kinolanasi hesumowa kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-08.jpg?direct&}}

Ide okodoka Anna mangelasi koybamowa kisasake. Adumtte Hawatte mangela miminimala košeriya bubiya ambos kinolake. Innakin fesumabbu kwa bubiya mangela kiminiki Annakin battaske. Woynabbu, kwa bubiya Anna abaya kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-09.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta Anna laga kwe bubiyesi anda kimeki ikka ellokowa kišoke, woynabbu Yesusi kokobinima kwe Annette arita woyniyomunanditta mangeliye sabbatasi akura oytenni.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-10.jpg?direct&}}

Mangeleye sabbatasi kona bayine nišano bala elilikike. Anna dama aneyala isuna dada gon ninake. Anna damowa aneyala isuna dadowa udeyabbu Yesula iske. Yesu dargadelala kibalemowalle akkureyowana kikke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-11.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu mangelana iminimme, Iša ditta mangeleyana lagala kosima kwa mangelana woleys kolanasi koyaki fiteta udumala kiša kasonasi ketawake. Anna kwa bubiyasi mangela anda šakafa kiminiyyana samanasuna ayšisi Yesu uliya fitetosumasi.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-12.jpg?direct&}}

Mina mayde niminimabbu nokofurunasi šonunni. Annatte kotengena nimininasi datellobbuna kosimme. Yesu biloka mangeleye kiniše kenolana šosuna. Yesu Anna kiša kišamowana, udeyabbu dargadelala kibalemana, Anna balakin kikas kibomowassina nokobininadada.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-13.jpg?direct&}}Anna, Yesusi kokobinimena Andiya kišamowa kobin kokama kwe bubiyasi ufuruna. Ditta Abure kosano bare, adda kišano kiša, adikiša kišano tamfeda, okodoka kosima suka dina kwa kišayana Yesusi kobinimma kishaya Anna ufurunni. Anna emeyki Yesusi nokobinimabu enatte kotengena kinanasi niyatoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-14.jpg?direct&}}Yesu nokobininasi kowwa kebin goske. Yesu Messiya Anna kiša Elloka kišama nekobinibe? Mangeloke nišamana, Anna akkura keyana dade nišama nokobinibe? Yesu mangeleye batas kolanasidabbu dargadelala kibalema nokobinibe?

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-15.jpg?direct&}}

Yesusina, Unu enasiddabbu kiminimowassi nokobiniya ena Kristiyana nišake! Sadalla kinna mannodiya šomowakinna Anna mannoda leledinowatta kekoke. Anna, ašosuma damaneyana mineyana, mangelina tabilowana oleys kolaki, alala kišama tabila tama kesoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-16.jpg?direct&}}

Ena Kristiyana nišayya, Yesu iminima minowa sabbatasi Anna mangeleyesi samanas kewike. Tama, Anna abaya dittiya kodiya kekelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-17.jpg?direct&}}

Ena Anna kodiyana Yesu harana nišamabbu, Yesu esasama sasowaye fayyanuna. Kristiyana nišayana mangela nimininasi darabe olina suna, iša ditta Anna amanatanno mangeleye oratouuyya samanas kewina. Mangeleyesi nibata nifalena helatana kesona.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-49-18.jpg?direct&}}

Anna daganunasina, Awriya kosan nokosasanasina, Kristiyanellette samarabbu gajouunasina, ide imin kesoma bubiya ellesi nisasanasi kesasa goske. Innaye bubiye Unutte tukula kišama kotengena ninanasi kenadadina.

Roma 3:21-26; 5:1-11; Yohanne 3:16; Marko 16:16; Kolosse 1:13-14; 2 Korinto 5:17-21; 1 Yohanne 1:5-10 kin kokama Anna awra fala.
