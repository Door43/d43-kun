====== 6. Anna Isaksi Darka Kišoke. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-01.jpg?direct&}}

Abraham adikišos kišamala Isak kwa anda hira kiddike. Woynabbu Abraham hariye kin ellasi,\\
ikka Isak darka hen kiko kišonasi duguliye sukata kisameke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-02.jpg?direct&}}

Harowa kosoda gera kulabbu Abraham dugule gon gomma lagowa kike. Anna Abraham\\
harowassi Rebekkata kikki maroske. Rebekka Abraham mišama ikkakiša ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-03.jpg?direct&}}

Rebekka ašiyesi iwiki harowatte Isakta gasunasi gomatoske. Wagowa kimala Isak idiginke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-04.jpg?direct&}}

Fanaka gera kulabbu Abraham ibalno, Anna Abrahamtte mikosoma ñela sowa Isakta šas\\
kolake. Anna Abrahamsi šos kikela kolannima košanowa ñelasa kišo kišike. Iša ditta Isak\\
darka Rebekka deda kišinasi šosumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-05.jpg?direct&}}

Isak Annata dagasso Rebekka unukin dede ibe katisunasi Anna heda kišake. Dede bariye\\
ininiña kasala mikosimiya ayniya bile mibacke. Rebekka Inna kasiya kala kišama damanena\\
aykiša dabbu Annasi kokelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-06.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Rebekkasi, “inna kaseyala mikosima dede barenamekin agare bare miša mimona, ayniya\\
bile mibacina ide andowa damadowa hara kišana”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-06-07.jpg?direct&}}

Rebekka kiše mikošimala andowa biba ide uliya kimoka košino “Esaw”da kosoke. Damadowa\\
Esaw ocokonasi kibinki košike kidiya,“Yakob” da kosoke.

//Nena fala 24:1-25; 26. Kin kokama Anna awra Fala.//
