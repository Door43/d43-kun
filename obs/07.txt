====== 7. Anna Yakobsi Kišodima ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-01.jpg?direct&}}

Dedowame mitomišima kulabbu, Yakob iniñatte itigodda; mišama Esaw akkoyšandeya.\\
Rebeka Yakobsi anda kimeyke ditta Isak Esawsi kimeyke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-02.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella Esaw koyšakin kidemala lila anda kililino, Esaw Yakobsi, “toro nussuma\\
ñadadenakin damade kaso”ske. Yakob, “ekkena adigila nišamabbu nitena šodowa kaso”ske.\\
Woynokala Esaw adigiliya šoda kišoke. Yakobna ussuma ñadadowakin Esawsi kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-03.jpg?direct&}}

Isak Esawsi kišodina heske; šodowa ana Rebekkatte Yakobtte mišaki Isaksi mimamijike.\\
Yakob mišama Esaw kokkaloki Iwa Isakta kuwakike. Isak ito kišama sabbatasi intinasi\\
šosummeno, Yakob mišama Esaw sesowa koweki, koniyelana seriyelana sesa agala kofo seges\\
kiki Isaksi imamajike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob Isakta seges kiki, “aba Esaw deya; inne nawkema ašodi”ske. Isak, sesa kimowassi kafas\\
kintiki, sesiyowaye šiñkas kintiki Esaw kallabbu kikki išodike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-05.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob, adigiliya majana šodana kimamaji kuful kikama sabbatasi, Esaw Yakobsi koybaki Iwa\\
ibalema badasi kiya fasunasi lawaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-06.jpg?direct&}}

Išaditta iniña Rebeka Esaw lawetowa itakke. Woynabbu Rebekkana Isakna gomatonki\\
kebesiyette woyniyosunasi suka gerata misameno gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-07.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob galle artesi Rebeka kebesiyette goske. Woyna kulabbu idiginiki adde šeb’anala barena,\\
kišellana kišike. Anna, bura anda kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-08.jpg?direct&}}

Galle šebbare gosumala, Yakob itiya dinena asaniyena šigidiyena kikaki Kanana lagata kideke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-09.jpg?direct&}}

Yakob, mišama Esaw ayteya aya fasunanda lawaski kayloke. Woynabbu maydab kibin\\
kikanasi hariyekin asamade kinolaki šodada kalla šigida udumala kokiki Esawsi koki kosonasi\\
kisamake. Šigidowaye kika gamma Yakob harowaye, “hareya Yakob inna šigidenaye kisame\\
kesoke. Ununa badañokasi koñña”nki, osasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-07-10.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Esaw ašimiya mišama ambos kiminimimowassi samanas kišoke. Imme gadibakin\\
gadibata mikolemasi gogon mišake. Yakob Kanana lagala sullumabbu goske. Woyna kulabbu\\
Iwa Isak ibalno, Yakobtte Esawtte miki kabbarenke. Anna, Abrahamsi ñela sa kišowamowa,\\
tamana Yakobta šas kolake.

//Nena fala, 25:27-33,20. Kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
