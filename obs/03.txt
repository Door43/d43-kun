====== 3. Akura Biya ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-01.jpg?direct&}}

Fanaka gera kulabbu lagala agara arta fannanke. Ime akkeda anda bayena mongorena kosake.\\
Mengela mina arta fawsuma kintimala, Anna lagasi akura biya kele kibal kinolana kimintike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-02.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Nowa fanokowallekokosima kwe kin kwa alala fogadakin battaski woyniyosuma\\
kišamasi Anna wala kondora kiteke. Anna lagasi akura biyabbu kibal kolanowa kudaki Arka\\
kitanasi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Nowasi Itana arkowa gerida, solosolata metre 140, ide sasidiya metre 23,baddata\\
metre13.5 kisasano itake. Nowa Arkowa elabbu awsas kitaki, kodore satte kinnama\\
kišaki,dama artena, badda kofodana, okodoka finesterena kinnake. Anna arkowabbu nowasina\\
itiya dinesina lagala kokosima sosona bubiyekinna akura biyowakin kufurunasi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-04.jpg?direct&}}

Nowa Annam isasa isasamowasi fayyaske. Ikkesattiyette košaki Annam isasama tabilowabbu\\
Arkowa awsanke. Arkowa illiyab anda kišama sabbatasi imalanasi galla arta kikke. Nowa\\
agaresi akura biyowa yomma ana Annata kodena kisota kinake; iša ditta agare amajomumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-05.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Nowasi imesina arkoewalle koluna sosonenasi kišana ňada kowawinasi kisasake. Data\\
bubiya kussuma kulabu nowa darkiyana ikkena ikke darke sattena arkowatta koluna fanaka\\
yomowa kisasake. Arkowalle kolume bubiyebbu kwe konsatte.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-06.jpg?direct&}}

Anna akura biyakin kokofurunasi sosone bubiyekinnna ete kinna šigide kinna butette\\
šinnetteda kitafaraki nowata kisameke. Fitetasi kosana šigedekin kontabare šigide jinisa\\
arteki nowata arkowatta kisameke. Bubiye arkowale kolumala Anna ayniya arka dagga kiseke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woynakin akura biyowa išiňana kibinke. Mintitta uye 40 awade 40 ñora anda išinke. Laga\\
kasakakina irawekin biya amuske. Lagala kosima bubiyasi akura biyowa mes kolake. Aya\\
gerellana lakasumma kifo kolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-08.jpg?direct&}}

Tur arkowa kala koluma kwowayena sosonowayena ditta lagala kokosima šukina bubiya\\
ucuke. Arkowa biya baddab gaski kasiyakala kokosima šukine bubiye kufurke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-09.jpg?direct&}}

Ñorowa saynasumala, arkowa tele kussumeta biya baddokala soliske. Woyna badasi biyowa\\
dogolada kibinke. Amelella arkowa aya tunkušala lakaske. Attile laga biya ifomiya luske. Tele\\
satte badasi aye tunkuše okkontina kobinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-10.jpg?direct&}}

Uye šebsalle kulabu biyowa šus kilabebessa itakenasi gabara kisameke. Gabarowa attana\\
ottana fittiske ditta waga alaba kitenasi šosumme

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-11.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi uba kisameke ubowa kale hakes kikana waga itmmeno Nowata kideke. Uye\\
kontabare badasi ikaski ubowa kisameke. Ubowa miyala lila ela durfa kikki ikas kide kike,\\
woyna fanakowa biya dogolas kišamowano ele tunteda kobinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-12.jpg?direct&}}

Uye kontabare badasi nowwa minda asatta Ubowa kikask kisameke. Iša ditta laga ilab kišano\\
kalena waga kitema sabbatasi aši kalla ide kimme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-13.jpg?direct&}}

Tere bare kulabbu Anna Nowasi, “enana iteya dinena Arkowakin esa. Ekošeri fannanki Lagala\\
wamu”ske. Woynabbu Nowa itiya dinette arkowaki ošake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-14.jpg?direct&}}

Nowa arkakin kišamala kanya kewaki fitetasi kosana šigidowayekin kikoki Annasi fiteta kimin\\
kišoke. Anna fitetiyowabbu gogoski Nowasina itiya dinesina kišodike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-15.jpg?direct&}}

Anna akeske, “kwe mina baya kominima sabbabbu lagasi nakas nabal nawlannimasi nada\\
nawa gonake. Kwe tanfediya goddakin kobinki mangelomuyana inna kin kulata nakaski akura\\
biyabbu nabal nanolanni.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-03-16.jpg?direct&}}

Anna andiňňa nada kuluki alemata kišanasi malanga sanga kidore. Malanga sanga dimala\\
kuyala kontimala Anna agariyette kuluma nadowa kayyana kiminke.
