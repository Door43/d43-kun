====== 18. Israel Mannoda Barela Ges ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-01.jpg?direct&}}

Galle arte kulabbu Lugusa Dawit ibalno ikka Solomon Israelsi mannosuna kibinke. Anna Solomonsi konti kišoki, “data bubiyakin baddata ay noggošano”ski kokelake. Solomon šibaba kišonasi kokelano, Anna woynabbu gogoski laga sasala kokosima ašibabe bubiyekin ašibaba kolake. Solomon taka andina kinama ide alaloda sasa šibabina kišake; okodoka Anna abura anda kolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-02.jpg?direct&}}

Yerusalemla, Solomon fiteta itanda iwa Dawit lawaski itana datena summasuma itowa kitake. Agare Kola Tenda itowa udabbu tama fiteta itowalle gajodana fitetana Annasi koki kosoke. Anna itama fiteta itowalle koyteki olle agariyette gos kinake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-03.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta Solomon kwe dibe darke arte kimeyke. Darkenaye kidiginiki Annasi fayyas kišomme, Bubiyebbu 1,000 kosane darke kimeke! Darkiyowaye artiye kadana lagakin kolomeno lakilema anniya ummiyena kika koloki gajo koinake. Solomon ito kišamala darkiye annowayesi gajoske.።

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-04.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Solomonbbu košišike. Solomon amanata kišamma sabbatasi Anna akkuriya Solomon ibalema badasi Israelsi barela ges kolanowa kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-05.jpg?direct&}}

Solomon ibalemala ikka Rehoboam lugusa kišake unu urfitta gos kinake. Israele lugusa kišamowa kotalali kosonasi kokobuce koloke. Iwa Solomon gudurata sana arta kišo kinamana fuggara arta kutuno ola koynamana Solomonla urfa konoda koyna koynamowana Rehoboamsi osasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-06.jpg?direct&}}

Rehoboam urfiya kotomakin fesumabbu agarowassi, “awa Solomon gudurata sana taggima kasokenki lawanke aba woynakin šašama nasobbu mesanana; awakin šašamana akkura nayana”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-07.jpg?direct&}}

Israel košera kure šebowaye Rehoboam bagana ganke. Košere barowame lugusowassi amanata kosaki okosodake. Inna košere barenaye Yuda mannoda mišake

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-08.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Rehoboam bagana femma košere šebowaye, Yeroboam kotawaki lugusiya kolake. Aranta minda gatallebbu gonki Israel mannodanbabbu kokkidake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-09.jpg?direct&}}

Yeroboham Annasi baganoski agare mangela komininasi doroske. Yuda sukala kosima fiteta itowatta koliki Annasi gajomuna udabbu, agare gajomunasi anna kokale bare kile kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-10.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda mannodana Israel mannodana  abaya mišaki minda arta ayniya bile obaci koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-11.jpg?direct&}}

Israel mannoda lugusomma luguse bubiye mongora gon koynake. Inna lugusenayekin artiye banbariyowalle lugusa  godda hemma Israele iyafan koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-12.jpg?direct&}}

Israel mannodala Luguse bubiyena agare artena anna ummiyesi kokošodi koynake. anna ummiye gajodiya damana, kofala kokofalimabbuna šada šanki ikkesi fitatana kite koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-18-13.jpg?direct&}}

Yuda luguse Dawit košere gon koynake. Inna lugusenayekin elle lušabbu marommena Annasi gajommena gon koynake. Artowaye lugasa bayena mongorena gon koynake. Okodoka anna ummiyesi gajommena okos koynake. Lugusa elleyde anna akoñeresi ikkena fiteton kišomena okoske. Yuda agara artowaye artowayaye Annasi baganonki anna ummiyasi gajo koynke.

//Lugusa 1-6, 11-12 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
