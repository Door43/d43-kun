====== 13. Anna Israelette Ñelasa Kokosoma. ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-01.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israelesi Bahara bibakin kika maroski badummabbu kikaki Sinay alyata kikike. Inna alyena Muse šarbatala toma kintima alyowa deya. Israele inna alyena mindala tendiye ton kikake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Musesina Israelesina, “Abasi fayyan kasoki, ñelaña sasina goññemuya Añña bibay mišana. Eme mannoda kašše mišaki haron kasona ide agara keddusena mišana”ski isasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-03.jpg?direct&}}

Uye satte badasi, Anna inna agarenaye aširmabbu akkosse kosama uyala, kudududabbuna mikadabbuna dudabbuna mena anda kinama fakara menabbuna Sinay alyata iske. Woyna alyowatta agusunasi Muse bilokasi yes kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Anna Israelesi ñelasa kišoki, “Aba Eyitto saba goddakin nanola nakoma woyniya gonama Anna ndeya. Woynabbu anna bodesi mikošodime”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-05.jpg?direct&}}

Anne meley mesusuki gajomme; ayšisi Aba Anneya Anna akkolugamma, kidaña ummiyabbu akidame. Uya akontabarowa,  uya keddusamma goññemu. Abbariya uye kontalle mesana mekki uya akontabara haken mekki abasi mekkaya kasona uya ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-06.jpg?direct&}}

Ewasina eneñasi añkatoda kišo, kwa niya fanume, awanonume, nunyame, niñerame, išigoleya, darka kišano, itiya kišano, iñña kišama data bubiya nikanasi durmenume.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-07.jpg?direct&}}

Anna inna fanede šebenayesi uga takase barela kilattaki Musesi kišoke. Okodoka,  fanede artena okosodandada komara tabilena Musesi kišoke. Agarenaye fanedenaye fayyan tillamuya Anna išodinana okodoka dariskišona kišama amanida kišoke. Fanedenaye fanki tillamumma kišayya, Anna akkura kiyanowana kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-08.jpg?direct&}}

Okodoka Anna Israele otana hesuma tendowa aykedabbu kotanibessa derñadiya udumaliya kisasake. Tenda itowasi Kola Tenda nda kokida koynake. Tendowa hoynada sesab magan mišama magade bare kinnake. Anna woyna hoynada sesowabbu domma wagowalle gos kinama sabbatasi, otta šas kuluna edasa kinama kaše adiyokowa deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-09.jpg?direct&}}

Kwella Anna fanede lawsuma kišayya, Kola tendowa kekala kosima fiteta kanyowatta, fitetasi kišana šigida kiki kinake. Kašowa šigidowassi iteki fiteta kanyowa baddasi kutuno koñames kinake. Šigidowa kokoba kwowa kusullumaki Anna kekala lušos kola kinake. Muse mišama Aronna Aron košeresi Kaša kosa kosananasi kinatawake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-10.jpg?direct&}}

Agarowaye bubiye, “inna Annam asoma fanedenayesi tilladina, Annokasi gajod kakošodina Annokasi kakkatawame kasana”nki gomatonke. Iša ditta Annasi fayyad kasonanki gomatommakin fanaka angude kulabbu fogada anda kominke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-11.jpg?direct&}}

Muse uya artesi Sinay alya baddala Annatte kidarmike. Agarowaye Unusi okofara kokoski amanida komintiki, Aronta dahabe kika koliki gajomuna anna gata kile kišonasi kokkelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-12.jpg?direct&}}

Aron dahabowayebbu aššakiša gata kinama anna ummiya kile kišoke. Agarowaye fayyaditta anna ummiyowassi gajonki fitetana komin kosoke! Anna ominima mangelenabbu anda košišiniki, iya kinolana matareba kolake. Ditta Muse Annata dagasso Anna Muse dagada kitikki iyammiya kiwike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-13.jpg?direct&}}

Muse Sinay Alyakin iskoki gajomuna anna gata kintimala anda košišiniki, fanede šebe minam ugowamme fas taws kinolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-14.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Muse aššakiša gata kinama anna ummiyowassi kaws kolaki biyala killaki agarowayesi kišono onoke. Annana agarowayeta damiya kisameno imekin kwe arte obalke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-13-15.jpg?direct&}}

Muse ikaski taws kinolamanda uga takasowaye udabbu fanede šebowaye kilattana uga takase bare kususu kikaki alyata agus kiki, Annasi agarowaye samanas kiwinasi dagaske. Annana Muse dagada kitikki samanas kiwike. Muse fanede šebowaye uga tamela kika koke.Woynakin Anna Sinay alyakin ñelasa kuwaki nasonansuma lagowatta kika maroske.
