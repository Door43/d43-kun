====== 19. Edesse Fala ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-01.jpg?direct&}}

Israel agaresi woyniyodiya fanakala Anna edesse kisame kišo kinake. Edesse Annakin edetta kokki agariyesi kisasa koyname deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-02.jpg?direct&}}

Akab Israella mannosuma fanakowalle Eliya Edessa gos kinake. Akab agariyesi baalda kokidama anna añerowassi gajomunasi dorosuma kwa mongora gos kinake. Eliya Akabsi, “aba ñora isunannaki nawdanata Israella ñorana uba biyana kosinni.”ske. Inna awrena Akabsi anda kišišinike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-03.jpg?direct&}}

Akab Eliyasi iya fasuna hesso, Anna Eliyasi laysunasi selela kinama badummata kisameke. Molalana sudalana gabarebbu añgerana nyana koko koso koynake. Akab askariye kikano Eliyasi henke ditta oytenasi šomumme, lagowalle lilana anda tagiske subena okolab kosake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-04.jpg?direct&}}

Woynabbu Eliya išigole sukata kigaske. Inna sukelalla darka solakosella ikkette ñora kosimmowabbu koma lilowabu ñadadiya komalanasi kiddi kike, iša ditta Eliyasi miggommo Anna  hawa išidadakin lilana gibakin komalamma kimin kišono okosaleke. Woyna lilafanakowa bubiya ime ñada dawmumme; Eliyana galla artesi olle goske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-05.jpg?direct&}}

Galle sattena sarana kulabbu, Anna Eliyasi ñora isuna kišama Israel Lugusa Akabsi kisasanasi kisameke. Akab Eliya kintimala “tagama asasa, allele nokoskele?” ske. Eliyana ikas kišoki “tagama nikoma ena deya! Anna amanowa, niwiki baalsi gajonke. Tama agareyesi Karmel alyata kubuce” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-06.jpg?direct&}}

Israele mannodala gomma agarena baal edesse 450 Karmel ayata kokobuceke. Olle  Eliya agarowayesi, “ašik aykadditano lawete barela mefegeda mešiññino? Gajodindadowa Anna kišayya Unusi haromu Baal anna amana kišayya unusi haromu!”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Eliya baal edessesi, “ayla keteyki fitetasi segen keko. Iša ditta toma lemme abana okodoka  namini. Toma kisamemabbu kokeliñña  kikas kasomowa Anna amanamma”ske. Woynabbu baal edesse fitetasi kišanowa ossuke ditta tomoka lemumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-08.jpg?direct&}}

Batti  baal edesse baalta daganki, “ wo baal! itik kaso.”nke. uya gera awn daganki uliyana anderabbu kokkoylake ditta kasa oytemme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-09.jpg?direct&}}

Uyowa komaldala, Eliya Annata fiteta fitetosunasi seges kikike. olle kokosima agarowayesi fitetowa baddasi biya sala andikattebu segete kontalle kikiki ašik nyowana udowana, okodoka kanyowa higidowana nays kolanata lukumunasi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-10.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna badasi Eliya, “O Anna amana, Abrahamna Isakna Yakobna Anna, Ena Israel Anna nišamana aba hareya nasamana tamma uyena asobbu manti. Ena Anna amana nišama ketakenasi toma nisamemabbu kasa kaso.”ski dagaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-11.jpg?direct&}}

Woynokala, kulya baddakin toma iski nyowana, udowana, ugowayena, šišawowana, okodoka kanyowa higidala kos kutama biyowassina šis kola kiñameske. Agarowaye inna kontimala gadibabbu kokoyaki, “Anna andandeya! Anna unukandeya”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-12.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Eliya, “ baal edessekin ellana ejinnimasi domu”ske. Agare baal edessesi ibinki woyna wagowakin battan kiki kiya fanake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-13.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu Eliya Lugusa Akabsi, “ñora isunañamma etetandakin sukata kide”ske. Etetiya kulya badda kullusso ñoranda iske. Anna lilowassi utuno lakasso, Unu Anna amana kišama kitalali kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-14.jpg?direct&}}

Eliya badasi, Anna edessa kišanasi Elisada kokidama kwella ketawake. Anna Elisabbu faga mina arta kiminke. Fagiya minekin Namanda Israele abaye mara ella agala bada kinamasi kebaboma fagandeya. Unu Elisañña kitikki ebonasi Elisata kike. Elisana Yordan suba kiki minde kontobare biyowalle nis kišanasi Namansi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-15.jpg?direct&}}

Naman ekkenala Elisa woynakes kisasamasi kaymale sanende komin kiyano košišiniki isasamowa kiminina hes kutamme. Batti kuliski lawetiya kifegedaki Yordan suba biyowalle minde kontobaresi nis kišamala komalda agala badowakin koššo keboke! Anna ebaboke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-16.jpg?direct&}}

Anna edessa arte kisame kišo kinake. Ime bubiye anna ummiya gajoda owiki okodoka kwa bodesi alala minana  nyanyumodana  komininasi agariyesi isasake. Edessowaye agarowassi mina baya minakin kodemma kišayyana Annasi fayyamumma  kišayya, Anna mangeliyesi alalosunowana akkura kiyanowana abbaredasa kišo koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-17.jpg?direct&}}

Fanaka artowa agarowa Annasi fayyan kosomme. Minda artoka edessesi baya kimimki, Elliddela yana kiyafan koynake. Fanakellala edessa Yermiyasi olle kibalenasi adikata fan kotu kowi ganke. Adikowa komaldala karfa kinano nidanis kiggaske, Iša ditta lugusa neda yabbu kintiki ibalemma anabbu olliyesi Jermiyasi adikowakin ola kokonasi faneske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-19-18.jpg?direct&}}

Agaresubbu ukuyyana Edessowaye Annañabu awra owi koynamme. Urfiya kokofegedamma kišayya, Anna damiya kele kiyana kišamowa kisasake. Ide Messiya yona ñelasa kišomowa kutuno okkaya koynake.

//1 luguse 16-18, 2 luguse 5, Jermiya 38 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
