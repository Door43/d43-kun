====== 21. Anna Messiya Kisamena Amanida Kosoma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-01.jpg?direct&}}

Anna ekkenakin Messiya kisamenasi matareba kinake. Minda ekkenasi Adum kimme\\
Hawwatesi Messiya kisamena ñelasa kišoke. Anna Hawwa košerakin košima darma ansanga\\
sakkos kolanowa kisasake. Darma kokkaloki Hawwa kimamajimowa Sadalla deya. Ñela sowa\\
abbara Messiya yomala Sadallasi šillay kibat kifalenowa deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Abrahamsi lagala kokosima agare bubiye unullebbu šoda koytena kišama ñelasa kišo\\
kišike. Inna šodena koddi tillasunowa dubasi Messiyowa yomala kišana. Lagala kokosima\\
agarekinna košere bubiyena okofuruna šomune kinolana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Musesi dubasi kolona fanakela Anna unuyende edessella kibo kišona kišama ñelasa\\
kišoke. Inna kula fanaketa Messiya yona kišima amanidasa ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-04.jpg?direct&}}

Anna dawitsi košeriyekin ella lugusa kišaki Anna agaresi aritata marosunowa kišama amanida\\
kišo kišike. Inna Messiya Dawit košerekin konowa abbaresuma ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-05.jpg?direct&}}

Anna koyšiša tama agariyette kiminina kišama edessa Yermiyabu amanida kišo kišike. Inna\\
koyšišena Israel agarette Badummala kokosoma ñelasowamme. Koyšiša tamena Anna fanediye\\
agariye urfala kidorona, agariye ayniyoke Anniyasi kotakena, ide agariye kosana, Anna\\
mangeliyesi samanas kiwina. koyšiša tamena Messiyabbu tillasuna.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-06.jpg?direct&}}

Okodoka Anna isamema edesse Messiya eddessa, kaša, ide Lugusana kišanowa kodake.\\
Edessa ndimowa ella Anna awra kitikki Agaresi Annam udamowa kisasama ndeya. Messiyada\\
Annam nasameñña sumowa edessa dogoladitta ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-07.jpg?direct&}}

Israele kaše agarowaye udabbu mangele sabbatasi kokoyana akurowa udabbu Annata fiteta\\
segen kokina. Okodoka kaše agare iññabbu Annata dagamune deya. Messiya uliyasi fiteta\\
kolaki Annata sugus kikina Kaše andiya dogoladitta ndeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-08.jpg?direct&}}

Lugusa mannodellala mannoski agarasi alalosuma kwa ndeya. Messiya imamala Dawit\\
bambarala gosuma lugusa amana kišana. Laga sasala arita mannoski, kekabbu alalosumana\\
keka oššalosumana kišana.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-09.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Edesse, Messiyaññabu data artoke edessonke. Edessa Malaki Messiya yomma ana\\
Edessa andella yona kišama edessoske. Ide edessa Esaya Messiya kiša abiše kitakemmakin\\
košina kišama edessoske. Edessa Mika Messiya Betelihemla košina kišama kudake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-10.jpg?direct&}}

Edessa Esaya Messiya Galileyala woyniyosuan kišama, urfiya kokotemesina ititoki okodoka\\
saba kokobinimesi koybiša, okkolimesina ifira fanesuñña. Okodoka Messiya ika\\
kinikome,otikamma, ontimma, odana šomumma, gada gamumma agaresi inababona kišama\\
edessoske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-11.jpg?direct&}}

Edessa Esaya Messiyasi sababittabu kokkoybanowana baganomunowana edessoske. Edessa\\
ellelle Messiya koya fammowaye sesiyasi murka fada gen kokanowan kodiya bašaratosuna\\
kišanana anin kodake. Edessa Zakkariya kodiyanda bašaratosunowa asseta šolde šebsatte\\
bašaratasi jabis kosona kišama anis kudake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-12.jpg?direct&}}

Okodoka edesse Messiya baliya aykawlib kibaleññibessana kodake. Edessa Esaya kwe\\
Messiyasi jola tifon kotašenowana obayinowana oyanowana edessoske. Fogadellana\\
kiminimmiya masab kollenowana hayda andabu kibalena kišamowana kudake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-13.jpg?direct&}}

Edessellena, “Messiya mangela ellokana kinamma dogoladitta kišamowa, Kwelle mangeliyesi\\
koytena akurowa unu ikki bala kibalena, Messiya koyama akurowabbu Annatte kwette agasala\\
sulluma kišana, Inna sababenabbu Messiya haydowa Anna niyata kišamowa” nda kodake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-14.jpg?direct&}}

Eddesse Messiya ibalenowana ide Anna obalemekin ikas kibona kišamowana aninki edesson\\
košike. Anna Messiya balana kasaiya fedowanabbu mangeline kufuruna matarebowa tillaski\\
Ñelasa Tama kibiniññowa kodake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-21-15.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Messiyaññabu edessa artesi keka kola kišoke, iša ditta Messiya woyna edessowaye okos\\
koynama fanakowalle yomme. Komaldala Edessomma edesse awrowaye fanakowakin galle\\
400 kin kulabbu, Fanakiya šadiyasi Anna Messiyasi lagaata kisameke.

Nena fala 3:15,12:1-3, kosada 18:15, 2 Samuel 7; Yermiya 31; Esaya 59:16, Daniel 7; Malaki 4:5;\\
Esaya 7:14; Mika5:2; Esaya 9:1-7, 35: 3-5, 61:53; Dawit Anana 22:18, 35:19, 69:4, 41:9,\\
Zekariya11:12-13, Esaya 50:6 Dawit Anane 16:10-11.
