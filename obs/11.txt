====== 11. Šada Furda ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-01.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Farawosi Israele  sakas kiwibu gamummale kišaya, kwe kinna šigide bute kinna adda adigila bubiyasi kiya fasunowa abbaredasa kišoke. Farawo itikkke ditta attile Annasi amajosumme ide fayyasumme.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna unusi kobinima kwa bubiyasi ikka  adda adigile kofurunasi tabila kussu kišoke. Kwale itiya dinida garma naca kotawa koteyke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-03.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Israelsi Garma naca kokobabbu daggiya udidda kofulunasina nyowasina ginta angeratte koli koñanasi faneda kišoke. Oñama  kulabbu Eyittokin kajarasi kokkosuke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-04.jpg?direct&}}

  - Israele Annam fanesuma bubiya kominke. Awada kišamala Anna Eyitto bubiya gaski adda adigila bubiya kiya kinolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-05.jpg?direct&}}

Israele ita kunduška bubiya kokobabbu kofuluma kišama sabbatasi, Anna itiyesi iwi šaske. Itowayela kokosima kwowaye bubiye okofurke. Israele garma naca kokoba sabbatasi kokofurke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-06.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Eyitte Annasi okobinimme fanediyasina goññemumme. Woyna sabbatasi Anna itiyesi iwi šasumme. Anna Eyittola kokosima adda adigila bubiya kiya kinolake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-07.jpg?direct&}}

Eyitte mabusa itala kokkollimekin kibiniki komalda Farawo kiša adigilata kobalke. Amadowakin fesumabbu Eyittola kokosima kwela imbana audna kišake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-11-08.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna awadowabbu Farawo, Musette Aronttesi kikidaki, “ Israele bubiye inna fanakenakin kekaki esagamu”ske. Eyitto  agarena Israele eteta lagiya kowi kosanasi šugunke.

//Kajara fala 11:1-12; 32 kin kokama Anna awra fala//
