====== 30. Yesu Agara Alufe Kussumesi Kisalema ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu apostolesi ora suka aññadiyeddeta koli samomunasi kisameke. Ime Yesu kosimowatta kokas kodemala, ominima bubiya ofali koske. Woyna kulabbu Yesu baharowa kokadaki dayšada kinama wagala Unutte kolibbu damade haken kokanasi kisasake. Woyna kulabbu aunkala agunki baharowa kokada kolike.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta aunkowabbu gamma, imesi kwe arte intike. Inna kwenaye ibata šamunasi baharowa dogonabbu kolajike. Yesuna olliye Bahara kadowa kolimala, kwe arte imesi ibat koli kuludake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg?direct&}}

Agarowa darkena dedena ikelammab 5,000 kokala abiše kinnake.Yesu agarowayesi kintima anda nes kiyake. Agarowaye Yesusibbu anada koynamma garmende deya. Woynabbu sasa kisasaki agasiyala kokosima badinesi inababoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg?direct&}}

Suda fanaka kiddimala olliye aken kosasake, “laga košomake inna igidiññenalle suka kosimme, ñadada koyte kota koñanasi isameb gamu”nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta Yesu olliyesi, “oñana ñadada ime eso!”ske. Ime okas kosoki, “woyna aykedib mamini, ikadib agarena bubiya masale? angere kussumokena Ise kiše barokena mayninake” nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg?direct&}}

Agarowaye šeb kussumeddabbu senala gomunasi kisasanasi Yesu olliyesi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu añgere kussumena Ise barowayena kikaki kulya baddata galaski Annasi ñadowayesi aftata kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg?direct&}}

Añgerowayesina Isowayesina kiteyki agarowaye kišonasi olliye kišoke. olliyena agarowayesi šigin kišono koddi kobañgeke. Agarowoye oñki obburke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna kulabbu olliyowaye agarowaye oñano kobañgemowa samate šebanala bare kowawike! Woyna ñadowa yomowa añgere kussumena Ise barenakin deya.

//Matteo 14:13-21; Marko 6:31-44; Luka 9:10-17, Yohanne 6:5-15 kin kokama Anna awra fala.//
