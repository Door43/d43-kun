====== 31. Yesu Biya Baddabbu Komi Gasuma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu olliyesi agarenaye kisamenata ime aunkabbu kada otta kokad kosanasi kisasake. Yesu agarowa kisamema kulabbu dagadasi alya fafata aguske. Woynalle awada farkata iñalliya gos dagakaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-02.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna fanakowalle Yesu olliye aunkowabbu ganki awada farkalana biyowa agasala kokoske. Sawita anda imeta fuffusuma sabbatasi anin gamunasi anda tagamonke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-03.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna fanakowalle Yesu dagada imalki olliyeta gaske. Biyowa baddala komiki aunkowatta gaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-04.jpg?direct&}}

Olliye Yesusi kontimala aširma kofakki anda kokkayloke. Yesu kayla kokkayloma kintike, “abama mekkaylome” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-05.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna fanakowalle petro Yesusi, “Andaña enale nišayya biya baddabbu nakomi ganaki enata nawnasi kuda” ske. Yesu Petrosi, “awa” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-06.jpg?direct&}}

Petro aunkowakin iski Yesuta kinasi biya baddowabbu gada kibinke. Ditta gera gasummiya wiyye Yesuta lida iwiki biya aranta karantamowattana sawita anda kutumowattana fayyaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-07.jpg?direct&}}

Batti Petro kayla kayloki biyata nida kibinke. Fanakowalle, “Andaña afuru”ski awske. Yesu Petrosi etetiyoka koniya lewes kibinki, “ena kobinna dogoladina ayšisi tirinno?”ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-31-08.jpg?direct&}}

Yesutte Petrotte aunkowatta mimiki agummala sawita fuffudiya iwi lakasso biyowana wis kišake. Yesu olliye anda kokomalle kosake. Yesusi gajonki, “Ena amana Anna Kiša”nki odake.

//Matteo 14:22-33; Marko 6:45-52; Yohanne 6:16-21 kin kokama Anna awra fala//
