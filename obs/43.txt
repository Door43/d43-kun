====== 43. Kwa Arte Kristo Yesu Badisase kosama ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-01.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu kulyata kogotama badasi olliye Yesu isasamende Yerusalemla kokoludake. Woyna fanakowalle akobinne bubiye dagadasi komintitta summan koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-02.jpg?direct&}}

Gallakelda mindella šada furdakin uya 50 badasi Yude Pentekosteda uya kogošinella kofurda koynake. Pentekoste, Yude adaba fanakala deya ofurdanowa. Yude pentecoste kofurdanasi laga sasakin Yerusalemta humman koli koynake. Inna gallenalle Yesu kulyata kogotamasibbu sambatella kinamala Pentocoste kofurdana fanakam yoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-03.jpg?direct&}}

Akobinne bubiye ellala summan kokosimala, kolalasi mena kinama huda husuma sawitende okobucema itowa waske. Woyna badasi toma ñela kokkaylome akobinnowaye bubiye ansangala kokkontike. Bubiye Aširmab wan kosaki ñela aññadiyedde kodana kobinke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-04.jpg?direct&}}

Yerusalemla kolutama kwowaye menowa kotikamala išama datowa kontinasi artabbu koloke. Akobinnowaye Anna mina mallata koyname iñña kodama kotikke. Inna kwe bubiye ñeliyeddabbu kotikamasi okomalleke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-05.jpg?direct&}}

Ellidde inna kwenaye ayfa iyakeda kodake ditta, Petro feski, “etik kaso! Inna kwenaye sakirnamumme! Anna edessa Yewelbbu komalda uyela Aširmaña natturuñña! suma edessa awrowa innabbu tillaske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-06.jpg?direct&}}

“O Israel kwame, eme aninki mentimendena metikamendena Yesu Anna guduratabbu faga mina andena fakkara andena kiminima ndeya. Iša ditta eme unusi misongoloke.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-07.jpg?direct&}}

“Yesu ibalema kišayyana Anna obalemekin ikas kiboke. Ide woyna ‘Kedduseyasi nabulala konoñontinasi niwini’suma edessa awrowa tillaske. Anna Yesusi woyniya kiska kibomowa ame šute deya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-08.jpg?direct&}}

“Yesu tama Anna Kiwa kona tokonata kogotake. Okodoka amanida kišo kišiminde Aširma Keddusasi isameke, tama išano metikki, menti gommeno bubiya Aširma Keddusam isana goske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-09.jpg?direct&}}

“eme Yesusi misongoloke. Ditta tama Anna Yesusi Andana Messiyana kolama kekabbu ketake.”

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-10.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna Petrosi fayyan koso kokosimowaye Awrowayebbu okosaneke. Woynabbu Petrosina kodiyesina kikidaki, “osamañame ay maminina dadono?”nki okkelake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-11.jpg?direct&}}

Petro, “Anna mangeleye samanasunasi mangeleye oratomu, Yesu kidabbu kekoduše. Woyna badasi Aširma Keddusa se kesonamma.” Ski ikas kišoke

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-12.jpg?direct&}}

Agara 3,000 kosane woyna Petro udamowa kotikki okobinimabbu Yesu olliye kosaki okodušeki Yerusalemla kosima košoda itowa dine kosake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-43-13.jpg?direct&}}

Woyna akobinnowaye mintitta apostole kosasa fayyan koynake. Fanakiyana samarabbu šan kolaki, Ellabbu koñki, samarabbu daganki, samarabbu Annasi kosaro koynake. Woynama bubiyakin bubiye gen kokki lin kokkonti koynake. Kwe arte maydodiyañña koda koynake, uyikelda kwe arte okobinki imeta kokotaf koynake.
