====== 17. Anna Dawitsi Ňelasa Kišoma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-01.jpg?direct&}}

Saul Israel Lugusa Ekkenowa deya. Unu Israele okogošama kokala gera sukka sukka ide girmatina ndeya. Saul Israelesi marosuma galla ekkenowayela Lugusa mayda gos kinake; fanake kulabbu Annasi fayyasumma  baya kišake, woynabbu Anna Amelellala  Saul udabbu lugusa kišana kwella ketawake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-02.jpg?direct&}}

Anna Dawitda kokidama Israel tamfedellasi Saul badasi Lugusa kišana ketawake. Dawit Betelihem suka dina šigidada ndeya. Dawit fanaka aññadiyiddela iwa garme kiyana koloma Morkena gubugubena kiya fas kinake. Dawit Annasi Amajosuma Afayyada kalasinana kwa alalana gos kinake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-03.jpg?direct&}}

Dawit masa abacaca andana amarana kišake. Dawit attile tamfeda kosimiyala kwa andikatta sukku sukka Goliyatda kokidamette kibacke. Goliyat masa kososona kinama abacaca iddira okodoka geridiya metre 3 kiddina. Anna Israele kufurunasiddabu Dawitsi idadino Goliyatsi iya fasake. Okodoka Dawit Israele abaye baddala bata fala kiteno Israele unusi osaroke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-04.jpg?direct&}}

Agare Dawitla koynama mowa kintimala Saul  kolugam ibinno Minda arta Dawit kiyana darbeske, woynabu Dawit layske. Amelellala Saul Dawit kiya fasunasi hes koski Dawit laysuma Dadowatta kike, iša ditta Dawitsi intimme. Dawit koriyala kos kutamasi intike ide iya fasuna hesuma kišayyana iyafasundada ditta iya fasumme. Woynakedakin, Saulsi kiya faski lugusa kišana hesummowa kišob intinasi Saul sesa ansala kiminti kikke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-05.jpg?direct&}}

Fanake kulabbu, Saul Masala koyano Dawit Israel Lugusa kišake. Dawit lugusa mayda kišamasi agare omeyke. Annana Dawitsi išodiki maydana kolake. Dawit masa arta kibacike Annana idadino Israel abayesi ibata kifalke. Dawit Yerusalemla kokosima košera aññadesi ugura kinolaki sukiya anda olle kidorke. Dawit mannoda fanakala Israele iddirena aburena gon koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-06.jpg?direct&}}

Dawit Israele bubiye Annasi gajomunasina fiteta komin kosonasina fiteta ita kitana heske. Israele galle muje sallesi Muse ita kišoma Annatte Kola Tendowalle gajonki fitetana komin koynake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-07.jpg?direct&}}

Ditta  Anna edessa Natansi, “ena masa kwa nišamasi šonki fiteta itowa nitanni, ditta ekka ita kasona. Ditta,  Enasi šoda anda našodina. Košeryeyekin ella agareñesi aritata mannosuna lugusa nabona”suma edetta kišoki Dawitta kisameke. Dawita košerekin Aritasi mannosunowa Messiya deya. Messiya lagala kokosima agaresi mangeliyekin kinolana Annabbu etawamowandeya.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-08.jpg?direct&}}

Anna ankatana šoda anda kišonasi ñelasa kišomala Dawit Inna ñela sena kitikamala  woynokala Annata asarana agawana kiki kišoke. Iša  ditta Anna inna ñela sena ikaddi tillasunissa itakemme. Israela Messiya yonata galla arta kokofarake, galle 1,000 kišana kalla kokofarake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-09.jpg?direct&}}

Dawita keka alalodabbuna amajodabbuna galla artesi maroske, Annana išodike. Ditta Dawit woyniyodiya komaldata kiddi gasumala mangela andella kiminke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-10.jpg?direct&}}

Amelella masa askare bubiye masa ajjata koli gammala, Dawit mannodiya kodora ita kin lisumala, darka fašella ula kufe kosima kintike darkowa kida Barseba.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-11.jpg?direct&}}

Dawit gadibiya wagowakin saros kolana udabbu, Darkowa koko kosona kwe kisano okono darkowatte niñiske. Niñimma kulabbu itiyata kikas kisameke. Uya angude šammala Barseba kata kišama Dawitta edetta kisameke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-12.jpg?direct&}}

Barseba abiša Uriyada kokidama Dawit abacaca fadabowayekin ellandeya. Dawit Uriyasi masa ajjakin ikida kikoki darkiya Barsebatte niñisunasi kisasa. Iša ditta Uriya Abacaca iddire ajjala kokosimiya unu ita ki hakes kikana hesumme. Woyna kintimala Dawit olliya Uriyasi  masa ajjata kikas kisameki Uriya ibalenasi masa tokoma kosima wagowatta kisamenasi askare antatowassi kisasake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-13.jpg?direct&}}

Uriya ibalemala Dawit Barsebasi idiginke. Batti, Barseba deda adda nawske. Anna dawit minowassi anda košišinike, woynabbu Edessa Natansi Dawit iminima mangelowa aykalli taggimibesa isasanasi kisameke. Dawit mangeliyekin kidemasi sabbatasi Anna samanas kiwike. Dawit tarfosuma woyniyodiya fanakowa bubiya tagama taggimebbu šasumalana, Annasi kisaki fayyas kišoke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-17-14.jpg?direct&}}

Iša ditta Dawit iminima mangelowasi akura, ikka mirka addowa ibalke. Okodoka woyniyodiya fanaka bubiyala itiya dine agasala baca kišake. Dawit kalmiyana anda dekes kišama fanake okos košike. Dawita Annala amanata subbu išamma kišayana,  Anna Dawitsi kišoma ñela sowassi amanataka goske. Woyna kulabbu Dawit Barsebatte dedellana adda nawnke kidiya Solomun da mikidake.

//1 Samuel10:15-19;24:31; 2Samuel 5:7;11-12 kin kokama Annawra fala.//
