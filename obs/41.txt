====== 41. Anna Yesusi Obalemekin Kiboma ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-01.jpg?direct&}}

Askare  Yesusi kosongoloma kulabu, Yude mare Pilatota koliki, “Woyna Yesumma añerowa, uye satte kulabbu nakas fenanas kišikema olliye oliki uliya konyunya kokki ikas feskemunnimasi, unyunyannimasi askare digomundada” nke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-02.jpg?direct&}}

Pilato, “aškara elleyde keki nabulowa maydab digomunasi keddoro”ske. Batti  nabulowa udala kotomama ugowa baddala omaša koyaki kwe oli konyunyannimasi digomuna askare kidorke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-03.jpg?direct&}}

Yesu kosongoloma aberkenowa uya yude sambatandeya. Yude inna uyenalle nabulata gamunasi doda. Sambata aberkena moliya darka angude okowawiki Yesu ulata koliki hallalama kotaf išin koyanasi kokkosuke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-04.jpg?direct&}}

kolalasi laga išigida anda kišake. Logoda-mikadende lesuma maliyaka kuyala kontike. Nabula kotoma kolutama ugowa kututura kolaki ugowa baddiyasi goske. Askara nabu adugabowaye okkaylo kosaki accende  lagata kokkake.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-05.jpg?direct&}}

Darkowaye nabulowatta kolimala maliyakowa, “mikkaylome. Yesu alle kosimme. Woynaña nakas fenañña sumende obalemeki woyniya feske! Limu  nabulowana kenti” ski isasake. Darkowaye nabulowatta Yesu ula kofal kolutama wagowatta; linke iša ditta  uliya olle kosimme!

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-06.jpg?direct&}}

Batti maliyakowa darkowayesi, “eliki olliyesi ‘Yesu obalemekina ikas feski Galileyata kenateke’nki esasa” ske.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-07.jpg?direct&}}

Darkowaye kaylabbuna gogoda andabbuna wan kosake. Woyna fala maydowa Yesu olliye kisasanasi ganke.

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-08.jpg?direct&}}

Darkowaye fala maydena kokki attile tabilala gan kokosimala, Yesu konti kišoki, “Mikkaylome, Galileyata nanatenowa ollañesi esasa ime olle abasi kantiññamma” ske.

//Matteyo27:62-28:15; Marko16:1-11; Luka24:1-12 Yohanne 20:1-18 kin kokama Annawra fala.//
